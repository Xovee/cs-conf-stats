SC 2022 Acceptance Rate

SC 2022 accepted 81 papers out of 320 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.3%.

The International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage and Analysis 2022 (35th) was held in Dallas, USA.

Compared with SC 2021, the acceptance rate decreased from 25.9% to 25.3%, and submissions decreased from 379 to 320.
