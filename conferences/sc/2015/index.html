SC 2015 Acceptance Rate

SC 2015 accepted 79 papers out of 358 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.1%.

The International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage and Analysis 2015 (28th) was held in Austin, USA.

Compared with SC 2014, the acceptance rate increased from 20.6% to 22.1%, and submissions decreased from 394 to 358.
