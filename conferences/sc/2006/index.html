SC 2006 Acceptance Rate

SC 2006 accepted 54 papers out of 239 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.6%.

The International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage and Analysis 2006 (19th) was held in Tampa, USA.

Compared with SC 2005, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.8% to 22.6%, and submissions decreased from 260 to 239.
