SC 1992 Acceptance Rate

SC 1992 accepted 75 papers out of 220 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 34.1%.

The International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage and Analysis 1992 (5th) was held in Minneapolis, USA.

Compared with SC 1991, the acceptance rate decreased from 38.6% to 34.1%, and submissions increased from 215 to 220.
