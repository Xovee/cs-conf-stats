SC 2010 Acceptance Rate

SC 2010 accepted 51 papers out of 253 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 20.2%.

The International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage and Analysis 2010 (23rd) was held in New Orleans, USA.

Compared with SC 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 22.6% to 20.2%, and submissions decreased from 261 to 253.
