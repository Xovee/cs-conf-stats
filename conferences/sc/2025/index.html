SC 2025 accepted 135 papers out of 623 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.7%.

The International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage and Analysis 2025 (38th) was held in St. Louis, USA.

Compared with SC 2024, the acceptance rate increased from 21.1% to 21.7%, and submissions increased from 470 to 623.
