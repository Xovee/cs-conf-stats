SC 1993 Acceptance Rate

SC 1993 accepted 72 papers out of 300 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.0%.

The International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage and Analysis 1993 (6th) was held in Portland, USA.

Compared with SC 1992, the acceptance rate decreased from 34.1% to 24.0%, and submissions increased from 220 to 300.
