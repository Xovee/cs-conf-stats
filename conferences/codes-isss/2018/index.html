CODES-ISSS 2018 Acceptance Rate

CODES-ISSS 2018 accepted 22 papers out of 86 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.6%.

International Conference on Hardware/Software Codesign and System Synthesis 2018 (16th) was held in Turin, Italy.

Compared with CODES-ISSS 2016, the acceptance rate decreased from 26.3% to 25.6%, and submissions increased from 80 to 86.
