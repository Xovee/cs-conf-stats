CODES-ISSS 2012 Acceptance Rate

CODES-ISSS 2012 accepted 48 papers out of 163 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.4%.

International Conference on Hardware/Software Codesign and System Synthesis 2012 (10th) was held in Tampere, Finland.

Compared with CODES-ISSS 2011, the acceptance rate increased from 28.2% to 29.4%, and submissions increased from 117 to 163.
