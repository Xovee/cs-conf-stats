CODES-ISSS 2013 Acceptance Rate

CODES-ISSS 2013 accepted 31 papers out of 111 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.9%.

International Conference on Hardware/Software Codesign and System Synthesis 2013 (11th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with CODES-ISSS 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.4% to 27.9%, and submissions decreased from 163 to 111.
