CODES-ISSS 2011 Acceptance Rate

CODES-ISSS 2011 accepted 33 papers out of 117 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.2%.

International Conference on Hardware/Software Codesign and System Synthesis 2011 (9th) was held in Taipei, Taiwan, China.

Compared with CODES-ISSS 2010, the acceptance rate decreased from 33.7% to 28.2%, and submissions increased from 98 to 117.
