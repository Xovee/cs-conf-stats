CODES-ISSS 2009 Acceptance Rate

CODES-ISSS 2009 accepted 51 papers out of 157 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 32.5%.

International Conference on Hardware/Software Codesign and System Synthesis 2009 (7th) was held in Grenoble, France.

Compared with CODES-ISSS 2008, the acceptance rate increased from 30.8% to 32.5%, and submissions increased from 143 to 157.
