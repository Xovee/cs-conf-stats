ACL 2024 Acceptance Rate

ACL 2024 accepted 943 papers out of 4,407 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.4%.

Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics 2024 (62nd) was held in Bangkok, Thailand.

Compared with ACL 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 22.1% to 21.4%, and submissions decreased from 4,864 to 4,407.
