ACL 2005 Acceptance Rate

ACL 2005 accepted 77 papers out of 423 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.2%.

Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics 2005 (43rd) was held in Ann Arbor, USA.

Compared with ACL 2004, the acceptance rate decreased from 25.3% to 18.2%, and submissions increased from 348 to 423.
