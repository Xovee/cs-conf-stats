ACL 2008 Acceptance Rate

ACL 2008 accepted 119 papers out of 470 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.3%.

Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics 2008 (46th) was held in Columbus, USA.

Compared with ACL 2007, the acceptance rate increased from 22.3% to 25.3%, and submissions decreased from 588 to 470.
