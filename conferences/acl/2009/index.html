ACL 2009 Acceptance Rate

ACL 2009 accepted 121 papers out of 569 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.3%.

Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics 2009 (47th) was held in Singapore, Singapore.

Compared with ACL 2008, the acceptance rate decreased from 25.3% to 21.3%, and submissions increased from 470 to 569.
