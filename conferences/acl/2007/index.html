ACL 2007 Acceptance Rate

ACL 2007 accepted 131 papers out of 588 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.3%.

Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics 2007 (45th) was held in Prague, Czech Republic.

Compared with ACL 2006, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.3% to 22.3%, and submissions decreased from 630 to 588.
