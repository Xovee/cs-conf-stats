ACL 2011 Acceptance Rate

ACL 2011 accepted 164 papers out of 634 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.9%.

Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics 2011 (49th) was held in Portland, USA.

Compared with ACL 2010, the acceptance rate increased from 25.1% to 25.9%, and submissions decreased from 638 to 634.
