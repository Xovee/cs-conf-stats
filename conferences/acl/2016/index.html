ACL 2016 Acceptance Rate

ACL 2016 accepted 231 papers out of 825 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.0%.

Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics 2016 (54th) was held in Berlin, Germany.

Compared with ACL 2015, the acceptance rate increased from 25.0% to 28.0%, and submissions increased from 692 to 825.
