OOPSLA 2007 Acceptance Rate

OOPSLA 2007 accepted 33 papers out of 156 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.2%.

ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Object-Oriented Programming, Systems, Languages, and Applications 2007 (22nd) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with OOPSLA 2006, the acceptance rate increased from 16.6% to 21.2%, and submissions decreased from 157 to 156.
