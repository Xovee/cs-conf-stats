OOPSLA 2021 Acceptance Rate

OOPSLA 2021 accepted 71 papers out of 205 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 34.6%.

ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Object-Oriented Programming, Systems, Languages, and Applications 2021 (36th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with OOPSLA 2020, the acceptance rate decreased from 36.1% to 34.6%, and submissions decreased from 302 to 205.
