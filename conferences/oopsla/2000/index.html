OOPSLA 2000 Acceptance Rate

OOPSLA 2000 accepted 26 papers out of 142 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.3%.

ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Object-Oriented Programming, Systems, Languages, and Applications 2000 (15th) was held in Minneapolis, USA.

Compared with OOPSLA 1999, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.7% to 18.3%, and submissions decreased from 152 to 142.
