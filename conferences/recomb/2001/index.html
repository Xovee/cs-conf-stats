RECOMB 2001 Acceptance Rate

RECOMB 2001 accepted 35 papers out of 128 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.3%.

Annual International Conference on Research in Computational Molecular Biology 2001 (5th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with RECOMB 1997, the acceptance rate decreased from 36.8% to 27.3%, and submissions increased from 117 to 128.
