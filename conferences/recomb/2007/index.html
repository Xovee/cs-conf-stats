RECOMB 2007 Acceptance Rate

RECOMB 2007 accepted 37 papers out of 169 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.9%.

Annual International Conference on Research in Computational Molecular Biology 2007 (11th) was held in Oakland, California, USA.

Compared with RECOMB 2006, the acceptance rate increased from 18.9% to 21.9%, and submissions decreased from 212 to 169.
