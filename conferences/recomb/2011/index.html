RECOMB 2011 Acceptance Rate

RECOMB 2011 accepted 43 papers out of 153 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.1%.

Annual International Conference on Research in Computational Molecular Biology 2011 (15th) was held in Vancouver, Canada.

Compared with RECOMB 2010, the acceptance rate increased from 20.5% to 28.1%, and submissions decreased from 176 to 153.
