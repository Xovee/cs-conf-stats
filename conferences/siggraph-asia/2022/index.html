SIGGRAPH Asia 2022 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH Asia 2022 accepted 150 papers out of 407 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 36.9%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques in Asia 2022 (15th) was held in Daegu, Korea.

Compared with SIGGRAPH Asia 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 34.1% to 36.9%, and submissions increased from 270 to 407.
