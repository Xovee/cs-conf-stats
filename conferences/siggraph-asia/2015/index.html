SIGGRAPH Asia 2015 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH Asia 2015 accepted 84 papers out of 302 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.8%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques in Asia 2015 (8th) was held in Kobe, Japan.

Compared with SIGGRAPH Asia 2014, the acceptance rate increased from 19.6% to 27.8%, and submissions decreased from 352 to 302.
