SIGGRAPH Asia 2012 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH Asia 2012 accepted 82 papers out of 326 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.2%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques in Asia 2012 (5th) was held in Singapore, Singapore.

Compared with SIGGRAPH Asia 2011, the acceptance rate increased from 21.8% to 25.2%, and submissions decreased from 330 to 326.
