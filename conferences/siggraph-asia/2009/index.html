SIGGRAPH Asia 2009 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH Asia 2009 accepted 70 papers out of 275 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.5%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques in Asia 2009 (2nd) was held in Yokohama, Japan.

Compared with SIGGRAPH Asia 2008, the acceptance rate increased from 18.4% to 25.5%, and submissions decreased from 320 to 275.
