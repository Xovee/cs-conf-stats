SIGGRAPH Asia 2019 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH Asia 2019 accepted 93 papers out of 309 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.1%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques in Asia 2019 (12th) was held in Brisbane, Australia.

Compared with SIGGRAPH Asia 2018, the acceptance rate increased from 30.0% to 30.1%, and submissions decreased from 353 to 309.
