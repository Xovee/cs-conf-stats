SIGGRAPH Asia 2020 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH Asia 2020 accepted 109 papers out of 305 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 35.7%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques in Asia 2020 (13th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with SIGGRAPH Asia 2019, the acceptance rate increased from 30.1% to 35.7%, and submissions decreased from 309 to 305.
