SIGGRAPH Asia 2016 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH Asia 2016 accepted 89 papers out of 300 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.7%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques in Asia 2016 (9th) was held in Macau, China.

Compared with SIGGRAPH Asia 2015, the acceptance rate increased from 27.8% to 29.7%, and submissions decreased from 302 to 300.
