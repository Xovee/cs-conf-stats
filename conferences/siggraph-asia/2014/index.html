SIGGRAPH Asia 2014 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH Asia 2014 accepted 69 papers out of 352 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.6%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques in Asia 2014 (7th) was held in Shenzhen, China.

Compared with SIGGRAPH Asia 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 25.2% to 19.6%, and submissions increased from 326 to 352.
