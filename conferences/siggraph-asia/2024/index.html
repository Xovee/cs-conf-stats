SIGGRAPH Asia 2024 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH Asia 2024 accepted 265 papers out of 899 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.5%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques in Asia 2024 (17th) was held in Tokyo, Japan.

Compared with SIGGRAPH Asia 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 33.7% to 29.5%, and submissions increased from 665 to 899.
