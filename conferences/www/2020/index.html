WWW 2020 Acceptance Rate

WWW 2020 accepted 219 papers out of 1,129 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.4%.

The ACM Web Conference 2020 (29th) was held in Taipei, Taiwan, China.

Compared with WWW 2019, the acceptance rate increased from 18.0% to 19.4%, and submissions decreased from 1,247 to 1,129.
