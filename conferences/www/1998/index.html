WWW 1998 Acceptance Rate

WWW 1998 accepted 54 papers out of 218 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.8%.

The ACM Web Conference 1998 (7th) was held in Brisbane, Australia.

Compared with WWW 1997, the acceptance rate increased from 23.2% to 24.8%, and submissions decreased from 276 to 218.
