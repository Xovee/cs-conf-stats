WWW 2019 Acceptance Rate

WWW 2019 accepted 225 papers out of 1,247 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.0%.

The ACM Web Conference 2019 (28th) was held in San Francisco, California, USA.

Compared with WWW 2018, the acceptance rate increased from 14.7% to 18.0%, and submissions increased from 1,155 to 1,247.
