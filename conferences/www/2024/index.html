WWW 2024 Acceptance Rate

WWW 2024 accepted 405 papers out of 2,008 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 20.2%.

The ACM Web Conference 2024 (33rd) was held in Singapore, Singapore.

Compared with WWW 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.5% to 20.2%, and submissions increased from 1,783 to 2,008.
