WWW 2016 Acceptance Rate

WWW 2016 accepted 115 papers out of 727 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.8%.

The ACM Web Conference 2016 (25th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with WWW 2015, the acceptance rate increased from 14.1% to 15.8%, and submissions decreased from 929 to 727.
