WWW 2022 Acceptance Rate

WWW 2022 accepted 323 papers out of 1,822 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.7%.

The ACM Web Conference 2022 (31st) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with WWW 2021, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.4% to 17.7%, and submissions increased from 1,736 to 1,822.
