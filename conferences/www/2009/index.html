WWW 2009 Acceptance Rate

WWW 2009 accepted 105 papers out of 823 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 12.8%.

The ACM Web Conference 2009 (18th) was held in Madrid, Spain.

Compared with WWW 2008, the acceptance rate increased from 11.7% to 12.8%, and submissions decreased from 880 to 823.
