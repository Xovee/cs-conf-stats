WWW 2010 Acceptance Rate

WWW 2010 accepted 91 papers out of 754 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 12.1%.

The ACM Web Conference 2010 (19th) was held in Raleigh, USA.

Compared with WWW 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 12.8% to 12.1%, and submissions decreased from 823 to 754.
