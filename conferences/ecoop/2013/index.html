ECOOP 2013 Acceptance Rate

ECOOP 2013 accepted 29 papers out of 116 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.0%.

European Conference on Object-Oriented Programming 2013 (27th) was held in Montpellier, France.

Compared with ECOOP 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 21.4% to 25.0%, and submissions decreased from 140 to 116.
