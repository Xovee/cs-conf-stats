ECOOP 2010 Acceptance Rate

ECOOP 2010 accepted 24 papers out of 108 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.2%.

European Conference on Object-Oriented Programming 2010 (24th) was held in Maribor, Slovenia.

Compared with ECOOP 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 21.4% to 22.2%, and submissions decreased from 117 to 108.
