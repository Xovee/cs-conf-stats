ECOOP 2024 Acceptance Rate

ECOOP 2024 accepted 47 papers out of 112 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 42.0%.

European Conference on Object-Oriented Programming 2024 (38th) was held in Vienna, Austria.

Compared with ECOOP 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 38.6% to 42.0%, and submissions increased from 57 to 112.
