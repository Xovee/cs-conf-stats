ECOOP 2001 Acceptance Rate

ECOOP 2001 accepted 18 papers out of 108 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.7%.

European Conference on Object-Oriented Programming 2001 (15th) was held in Budapest, Hungary.

Compared with ECOOP 2000, the acceptance rate decreased from 18.3% to 16.7%, and submissions decreased from 109 to 108.
