SIGSPATIAL 2012 Acceptance Rate

SIGSPATIAL 2012 accepted 35 papers out of 206 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.0%.

ACM SIGSPATIAL International Conference on Advances in Geographic Information Systems 2012 (20th) was held in Redondo Beach, California, USA.

Compared with SIGSPATIAL 2011, the acceptance rate decreased from 21.9% to 17.0%, and submissions increased from 160 to 206.
