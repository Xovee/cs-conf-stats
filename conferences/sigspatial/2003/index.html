SIGSPATIAL 2003 Acceptance Rate

SIGSPATIAL 2003 accepted 22 papers out of 82 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.8%.

ACM SIGSPATIAL International Conference on Advances in Geographic Information Systems 2003 (11th) was held in New Orleans, USA.

Compared with SIGSPATIAL 2002, the acceptance rate decreased from 34.6% to 26.8%, and submissions increased from 81 to 82.
