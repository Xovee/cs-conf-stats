SIGSPATIAL 2020 Acceptance Rate

SIGSPATIAL 2020 accepted 33 papers out of 149 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.1%.

ACM SIGSPATIAL International Conference on Advances in Geographic Information Systems 2020 (28th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with SIGSPATIAL 2019, the acceptance rate increased from 21.1% to 22.1%, and submissions decreased from 161 to 149.
