SIGSPATIAL 2005 Acceptance Rate

SIGSPATIAL 2005 accepted 31 papers out of 85 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 36.5%.

ACM SIGSPATIAL International Conference on Advances in Geographic Information Systems 2005 (13th) was held in Bremen, Germany.

Compared with SIGSPATIAL 2004, the acceptance rate increased from 33.3% to 36.5%, and submissions decreased from 90 to 85.
