SIGSPATIAL 2024 Acceptance Rate

SIGSPATIAL 2024 accepted 37 papers out of 122 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.3%.

ACM SIGSPATIAL International Conference on Advances in Geographic Information Systems 2024 (32nd) was held in Atlanta, USA.

Compared with SIGSPATIAL 2023, the acceptance rate increased from 20.1% to 30.3%, and submissions decreased from 189 to 122.
