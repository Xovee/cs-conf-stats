SIGSPATIAL 2006 Acceptance Rate

SIGSPATIAL 2006 accepted 31 papers out of 99 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 31.3%.

Compared with SIGSPATIAL 2005, the acceptance rate decreased from 36.5% to 31.3%, and submissions increased from 85 to 99.
