SIGSPATIAL 2019 Acceptance Rate

SIGSPATIAL 2019 accepted 34 papers out of 161 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.1%.

ACM SIGSPATIAL International Conference on Advances in Geographic Information Systems 2019 (27th) was held in Chicago, USA.

Compared with SIGSPATIAL 2018, the acceptance rate increased from 20.0% to 21.1%, and submissions increased from 150 to 161.
