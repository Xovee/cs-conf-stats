SIGSPATIAL 2017 Acceptance Rate

SIGSPATIAL 2017 accepted 39 papers out of 193 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 20.2%.

ACM SIGSPATIAL International Conference on Advances in Geographic Information Systems 2017 (25th) was held in Redondo Beach, California, USA.

Compared with SIGSPATIAL 2016, the acceptance rate increased from 18.5% to 20.2%, and submissions decreased from 216 to 193.
