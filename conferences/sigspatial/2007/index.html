SIGSPATIAL 2007 Acceptance Rate

SIGSPATIAL 2007 accepted 37 papers out of 129 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.7%.

ACM SIGSPATIAL International Conference on Advances in Geographic Information Systems 2007 (15th) was held in Seattle, USA.

Compared with SIGSPATIAL 2006, the acceptance rate decreased from 31.3% to 28.7%, and submissions increased from 99 to 129.
