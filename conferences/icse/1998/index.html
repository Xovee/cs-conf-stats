ICSE 1998 Acceptance Rate

ICSE 1998 accepted 41 papers out of 209 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.6%.

International Conference on Software Engineering 1998 (20th) was held in Kyoto, Japan.

Compared with ICSE 1997, the acceptance rate increased from 18.7% to 19.6%, and submissions decreased from 219 to 209.
