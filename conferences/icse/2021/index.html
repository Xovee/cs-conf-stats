ICSE 2021 Acceptance Rate

ICSE 2021 accepted 138 papers out of 615 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.4%.

International Conference on Software Engineering 2021 (43rd) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with ICSE 2020, the acceptance rate increased from 20.9% to 22.4%, and submissions decreased from 617 to 615.
