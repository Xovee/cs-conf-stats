ICSE 1999 Acceptance Rate

ICSE 1999 accepted 49 papers out of 260 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.8%.

International Conference on Software Engineering 1999 (21st) was held in Los Angeles, California, USA.

Compared with ICSE 1998, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.6% to 18.8%, and submissions increased from 209 to 260.
