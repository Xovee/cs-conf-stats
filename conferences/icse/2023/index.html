ICSE 2023 Acceptance Rate

ICSE 2023 accepted 207 papers out of 780 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.5%.

International Conference on Software Engineering 2023 (45th) was held in Melbourne, Australia.

Compared with ICSE 2022, the acceptance rate decreased from 28.5% to 26.5%, and submissions increased from 691 to 780.
