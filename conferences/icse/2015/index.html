ICSE 2015 Acceptance Rate

ICSE 2015 accepted 84 papers out of 452 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.6%.

International Conference on Software Engineering 2015 (37th) was held in Florence, Italy.

Compared with ICSE 2014, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.0% to 18.6%, and submissions decreased from 496 to 452.
