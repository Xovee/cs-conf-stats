ICSE 2009 Acceptance Rate

ICSE 2009 accepted 50 papers out of 405 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 12.3%.

International Conference on Software Engineering 2009 (31st) was held in Vancouver, Canada.

Compared with ICSE 2008, the acceptance rate decreased from 15.1% to 12.3%, and submissions increased from 370 to 405.
