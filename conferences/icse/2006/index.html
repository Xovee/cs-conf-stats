ICSE 2006 Acceptance Rate

ICSE 2006 accepted 36 papers out of 400 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 9.0%.

International Conference on Software Engineering 2006 (28th) was held in Shanghai, China.

Compared with ICSE 2005, the acceptance rate decreased from 14.0% to 9.0%, and submissions increased from 314 to 400.
