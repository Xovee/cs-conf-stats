ICML 2006 Acceptance Rate

ICML 2006 accepted 140 papers out of 548 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.5%.

International Conference on Machine Learning 2006 (23rd) was held in Pittsburgh, USA.

Compared with ICML 2005, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.3% to 25.5%, and submissions increased from 491 to 548.
