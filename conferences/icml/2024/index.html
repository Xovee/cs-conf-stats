ICML 2024 Acceptance Rate

ICML 2024 accepted 2,609 papers out of 9,473 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.5%.

International Conference on Machine Learning 2024 (41st) was held in Vienna, Austria.

Compared with ICML 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.9% to 27.5%, and submissions increased from 6,538 to 9,473.
