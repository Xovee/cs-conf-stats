ICML 2019 Acceptance Rate

ICML 2019 accepted 774 papers out of 3,424 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.6%.

International Conference on Machine Learning 2019 (36th) was held in Long Beach, California, USA.

Compared with ICML 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 25.1% to 22.6%, and submissions increased from 2,473 to 3,424.
