ICML 2015 Acceptance Rate

ICML 2015 accepted 270 papers out of 1,037 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.0%.

International Conference on Machine Learning 2015 (32nd) was held in Lille, France.

Compared with ICML 2014, the acceptance rate increased from 25.0% to 26.0%, and submissions decreased from 1,238 to 1,037.
