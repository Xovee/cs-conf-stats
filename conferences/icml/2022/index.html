ICML 2022 Acceptance Rate

ICML 2022 accepted 1,235 papers out of 5,630 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.9%.

International Conference on Machine Learning 2022 (39th) was held in Baltimore, USA.

Compared with ICML 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 21.5% to 21.9%, and submissions increased from 5,513 to 5,630.
