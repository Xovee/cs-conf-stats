ICMR 2019 Acceptance Rate

ICMR 2019 accepted 26 papers out of 83 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 31.3%.

ACM International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval 2019 (9th) was held in Ottawa, Canada.

Compared with ICMR 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 32.4% to 31.3%, and submissions decreased from 136 to 83.
