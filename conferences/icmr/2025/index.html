ICMR 2025 Acceptance Rate

ICMR 2025 accepted 221 papers out of 515 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 42.9%.

ACM International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval 2025 (15th) was held in Chicago, USA.

Compared with ICMR 2024, the acceptance rate increased from 41.6% to 42.9%, and submissions increased from 281 to 515.
