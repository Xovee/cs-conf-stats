ICMR 2012 Acceptance Rate

ICMR 2012 accepted 50 papers out of 145 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 34.5%.

ACM International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval 2012 (2nd) was held in Hong Kong, China.

Compared with ICMR 2011, the acceptance rate increased from 16.4% to 34.5%, and submissions decreased from 366 to 145.
