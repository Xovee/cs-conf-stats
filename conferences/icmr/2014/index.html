ICMR 2014 Acceptance Rate

ICMR 2014 accepted 42 papers out of 111 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 37.8%.

ACM International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval 2014 (4th) was held in Glasgow, Scotland, UK.

Compared with ICMR 2013, the acceptance rate increased from 33.3% to 37.8%, and submissions increased from 96 to 111.
