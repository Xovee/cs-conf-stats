ICMR 2021 Acceptance Rate

ICMR 2021 accepted 48 papers out of 134 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 35.8%.

ACM International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval 2021 (11th) was held in Taipei, Taiwan, China.

Compared with ICMR 2020, the acceptance rate decreased from 42.3% to 35.8%, and submissions increased from 78 to 134.
