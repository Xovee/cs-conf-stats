PLDI 2014 Acceptance Rate

PLDI 2014 accepted 52 papers out of 287 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.1%.

ACM SIGPLAN Conference on Programming Language Design and Implementation 2014 (35th) was held in Edinburgh, Scotland, UK.

Compared with PLDI 2013, the acceptance rate increased from 17.2% to 18.1%, and submissions increased from 267 to 287.
