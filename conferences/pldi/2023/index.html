PLDI 2023 Acceptance Rate

PLDI 2023 accepted 83 papers out of 281 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.5%.

ACM SIGPLAN Conference on Programming Language Design and Implementation 2023 (44th) was held in Orlando, USA.

Compared with PLDI 2022, the acceptance rate increased from 20.6% to 29.5%, and submissions decreased from 330 to 281.
