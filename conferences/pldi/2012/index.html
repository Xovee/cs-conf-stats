PLDI 2012 Acceptance Rate

PLDI 2012 accepted 48 papers out of 255 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.8%.

ACM SIGPLAN Conference on Programming Language Design and Implementation 2012 (33rd) was held in Beijing, China.

Compared with PLDI 2011, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.3% to 18.8%, and submissions increased from 236 to 255.
