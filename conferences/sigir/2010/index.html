SIGIR 2010 Acceptance Rate

SIGIR 2010 accepted 87 papers out of 520 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.7%.

International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval 2010 (33rd) was held in Geneva, Switzerland.

Compared with SIGIR 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 15.8% to 16.7%, and submissions increased from 494 to 520.
