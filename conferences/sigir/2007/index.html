SIGIR 2007 Acceptance Rate

SIGIR 2007 accepted 84 papers out of 490 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.1%.

International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval 2007 (30th) was held in Amsterdam, The Netherlands.

Compared with SIGIR 2006, the acceptance rate decreased from 18.5% to 17.1%, and submissions increased from 399 to 490.
