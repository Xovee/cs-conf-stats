SIGIR 2011 Acceptance Rate

SIGIR 2011 accepted 108 papers out of 543 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.9%.

International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval 2011 (34th) was held in Beijing, China.

Compared with SIGIR 2010, the acceptance rate increased from 16.7% to 19.9%, and submissions increased from 520 to 543.
