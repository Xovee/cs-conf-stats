SIGIR 2024 Acceptance Rate

SIGIR 2024 accepted 160 papers out of 791 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 20.2%.

International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval 2024 (47th) was held in Washington DC, USA.

Compared with SIGIR 2023, the acceptance rate increased from 20.1% to 20.2%, and submissions decreased from 822 to 791.
