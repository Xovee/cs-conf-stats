SIGIR 2014 Acceptance Rate

SIGIR 2014 accepted 82 papers out of 387 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.2%.

International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval 2014 (37nd) was held in Queensland, Australia.

Compared with SIGIR 2013, the acceptance rate increased from 19.9% to 21.2%, and submissions increased from 366 to 387.
