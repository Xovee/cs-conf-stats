SIGIR 2005 Acceptance Rate

SIGIR 2005 accepted 71 papers out of 368 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.3%.

International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval 2005 (28th) was held in Salvador, Brazil.

Compared with SIGIR 2004, the acceptance rate decreased from 21.7% to 19.3%, and submissions increased from 267 to 368.
