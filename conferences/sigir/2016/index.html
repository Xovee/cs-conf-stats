SIGIR 2016 Acceptance Rate

SIGIR 2016 accepted 62 papers out of 341 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.2%.

International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval 2016 (39th) was held in Pisa, Italy.

Compared with SIGIR 2015, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.9% to 18.2%, and submissions decreased from 351 to 341.
