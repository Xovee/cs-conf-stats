SIGIR 2001 Acceptance Rate

SIGIR 2001 accepted 47 papers out of 201 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.4%.

International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval 2001 (24th) was held in New Orleans, USA.

Compared with SIGIR 1999, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.4% to 23.4%, and submissions increased from 135 to 201.
