ICALP 1985 Acceptance Rate

ICALP 1985 accepted 49 papers out of 205 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.9%.

International Colloquium on Automata, Languages and Programming 1985 (12th) was held in Nafplion, Greece.

Compared with ICALP 1984, the acceptance rate decreased from 32.6% to 23.9%, and submissions increased from 141 to 205.
