ICALP 2010 Acceptance Rate

ICALP 2010 accepted 106 papers out of 389 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.2%.

International Colloquium on Automata, Languages and Programming 2010 (37th) was held in Bordeaux, France.

Compared with ICALP 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 34.1% to 27.2%, and submissions increased from 370 to 389.
