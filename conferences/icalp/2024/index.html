ICALP 2024 Acceptance Rate

ICALP 2024 accepted 153 papers out of 519 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.5%.

International Colloquium on Automata, Languages and Programming 2024 (51st) was held in Tallinn, Estonia.

Compared with ICALP 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.8% to 29.5%, and submissions increased from 443 to 519.
