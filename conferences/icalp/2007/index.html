ICALP 2007 Acceptance Rate

ICALP 2007 accepted 76 papers out of 242 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 31.4%.

International Colloquium on Automata, Languages and Programming 2007 (34th) was held in Wroclaw, Poland.

Compared with ICALP 2006, the acceptance rate increased from 26.8% to 31.4%, and submissions decreased from 407 to 242.
