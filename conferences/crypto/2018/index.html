Crypto 2018 Acceptance Rate

Crypto 2018 accepted 79 papers out of 351 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.5%.

Crypto: International Cryptology Conference 2018 (38th) was held in Santa Barbara, California, USA.

Compared with Crypto 2017, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.2% to 22.5%, and submissions increased from 311 to 351.
