CAV 2013 Acceptance Rate

CAV 2013 accepted 70 papers out of 209 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 33.5%.

International Conference on Computer-Aided Verification 2013 (25th) was held in Saint Petersburg, Russia.

Compared with CAV 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 31.4% to 33.5%, and submissions increased from 185 to 209.
