CAV 2016 Acceptance Rate

CAV 2016 accepted 58 papers out of 195 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.7%.

International Conference on Computer-Aided Verification 2016 (28th) was held in Toronto, Canada.

Compared with CAV 2015, the acceptance rate increased from 27.4% to 29.7%, and submissions decreased from 252 to 195.
