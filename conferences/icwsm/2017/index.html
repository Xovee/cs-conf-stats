ICWSM 2017 Acceptance Rate

ICWSM 2017 accepted 50 papers out of 372 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 13.4%.

ICWSM: International AAAI Conference on Web and Social Media 2017 (11th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with ICWSM 2016, the acceptance rate decreased from 17.0% to 13.4%, and submissions increased from 306 to 372.
