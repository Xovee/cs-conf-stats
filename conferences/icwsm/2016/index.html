ICWSM 2016 Acceptance Rate

ICWSM 2016 accepted 52 papers out of 306 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.0%.

ICWSM: International AAAI Conference on Web and Social Media 2016 (10th) was held in Cologne, Germany.

Compared with ICWSM 2015, the acceptance rate decreased from 18.7% to 17.0%, and submissions decreased from 343 to 306.
