Eurographics 2021 Acceptance Rate

Eurographics 2021 accepted 47 papers out of 129 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 36.4%.

Annual Conference of the European Association for Computer Graphics 2021 (42nd) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with Eurographics 2016, the acceptance rate increased from 29.8% to 36.4%, and submissions decreased from 161 to 129.
