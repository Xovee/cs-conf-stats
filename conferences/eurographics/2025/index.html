Eurographics 2025 Acceptance Rate

Eurographics 2025 accepted 75 papers out of 205 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 36.6%.

Annual Conference of the European Association for Computer Graphics 2025 (46th) was held in London, UK.

Compared with Eurographics 2024, the acceptance rate increased from 32.9% to 36.6%, and submissions increased from 161 to 205.
