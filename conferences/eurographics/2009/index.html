Eurographics 2009 Acceptance Rate

Eurographics 2009 accepted 57 papers out of 243 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.5%.

Annual Conference of the European Association for Computer Graphics 2009 (30th) was held in Munich, Germany.

Compared with Eurographics 2008, the acceptance rate increased from 19.3% to 23.5%, and submissions decreased from 300 to 243.
