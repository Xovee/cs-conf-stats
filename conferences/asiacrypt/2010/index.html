Asiacrypt 2010 Acceptance Rate

Asiacrypt 2010 accepted 35 papers out of 216 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.2%.

Asiacrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Application of Cryptology and Information Security 2010 (16th) was held in Singapore, Singapore.

Compared with Asiacrypt 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 13.7% to 16.2%, and submissions decreased from 300 to 216.
