Asiacrypt 2023 Acceptance Rate

Asiacrypt 2023 accepted 106 papers out of 375 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.3%.

Asiacrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Application of Cryptology and Information Security 2023 (29th) was held in Guangzhou, China.

Compared with Asiacrypt 2022, the acceptance rate increased from 27.0% to 28.3%, and submissions increased from 363 to 375.
