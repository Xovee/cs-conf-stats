Asiacrypt 2006 Acceptance Rate

Asiacrypt 2006 accepted 30 papers out of 314 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 9.6%.

Asiacrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Application of Cryptology and Information Security 2006 (12th) was held in Shanghai, China.

Compared with Asiacrypt 2005, the acceptance rate decreased from 15.6% to 9.6%, and submissions increased from 237 to 314.
