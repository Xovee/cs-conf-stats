Asiacrypt 2004 Acceptance Rate

Asiacrypt 2004 accepted 35 papers out of 208 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.8%.

Asiacrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Application of Cryptology and Information Security 2004 (10th) was held in Jeju, Korea.

Compared with Asiacrypt 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 17.6% to 16.8%, and submissions increased from 188 to 208.
