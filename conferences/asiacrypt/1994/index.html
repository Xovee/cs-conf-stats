Asiacrypt 1994 Acceptance Rate

Asiacrypt 1994 accepted 30 papers out of 94 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 31.9%.

Asiacrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Application of Cryptology and Information Security 1994 (2nd) was held in Queensland, Australia.

Compared with Asiacrypt 1991, the acceptance rate decreased from 40.2% to 31.9%, and submissions decreased from 97 to 94.
