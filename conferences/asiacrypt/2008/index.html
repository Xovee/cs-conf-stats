Asiacrypt 2008 Acceptance Rate

Asiacrypt 2008 accepted 33 papers out of 196 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.8%.

Asiacrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Application of Cryptology and Information Security 2008 (14th) was held in Melbourne, Australia.

Compared with Asiacrypt 2007, the acceptance rate increased from 14.8% to 16.8%, and submissions decreased from 223 to 196.
