Asiacrypt 2016 Acceptance Rate

Asiacrypt 2016 accepted 67 papers out of 240 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.9%.

Asiacrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Application of Cryptology and Information Security 2016 (22nd) was held in Hanoi, Vietnam.

Compared with Asiacrypt 2015, the acceptance rate increased from 25.5% to 27.9%, and submissions decreased from 251 to 240.
