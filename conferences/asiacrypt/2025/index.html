Asiacrypt 2025 Acceptance Rate

Asiacrypt 2025 accepted 143 papers out of 533 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.8%.

Asiacrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Application of Cryptology and Information Security 2025 (31st) was held in Melbourne, Australia.

Compared with Asiacrypt 2024, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.3% to 26.8%, and submissions increased from 433 to 533.
