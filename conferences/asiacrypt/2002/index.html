Asiacrypt 2002 Acceptance Rate

Asiacrypt 2002 accepted 34 papers out of 173 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.7%.

Compared with Asiacrypt 2001, the acceptance rate decreased from 21.6% to 19.7%, and submissions increased from 153 to 173.
