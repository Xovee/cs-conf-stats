FAST 2017 Acceptance Rate

FAST 2017 accepted 27 papers out of 116 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.3%.

USENIX Conference on File and Storage Technologies 2017 (15th) was held in Santa Clara, California, USA.

Compared with FAST 2016, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.5% to 23.3%, and submissions increased from 115 to 116.
