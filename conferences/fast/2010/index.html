FAST 2010 Acceptance Rate

FAST 2010 accepted 21 papers out of 89 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.6%.

USENIX Conference on File and Storage Technologies 2010 (8th) was held in San Jose, California, USA.

Compared with FAST 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 22.5% to 23.6%, and submissions decreased from 102 to 89.
