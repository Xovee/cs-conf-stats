STOC 2015 Acceptance Rate

STOC 2015 accepted 93 papers out of 347 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.8%.

ACM Symposium on Theory of Computing 2015 (47th) was held in Portland, USA.

Compared with STOC 2014, the acceptance rate decreased from 28.5% to 26.8%, and submissions increased from 319 to 347.
