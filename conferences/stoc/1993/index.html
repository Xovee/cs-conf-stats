STOC 1993 Acceptance Rate

STOC 1993 accepted 85 papers out of 227 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 37.4%.

ACM Symposium on Theory of Computing 1993 (25th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with STOC 1990, the acceptance rate increased from 27.6% to 37.4%, and submissions increased from 214 to 227.
