STOC 2001 Acceptance Rate

STOC 2001 accepted 83 papers out of 230 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 36.1%.

ACM Symposium on Theory of Computing 2001 (33rd) was held in Heraklion, Greece.

Compared with STOC 2000, the acceptance rate decreased from 46.7% to 36.1%, and submissions increased from 182 to 230.
