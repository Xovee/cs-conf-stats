STOC 1996 Acceptance Rate

STOC 1996 accepted 74 papers out of 201 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 36.8%.

ACM Symposium on Theory of Computing 1996 (28th) was held in Philadelphia, USA.

Compared with STOC 1993, the acceptance rate decreased from 37.4% to 36.8%, and submissions decreased from 227 to 201.
