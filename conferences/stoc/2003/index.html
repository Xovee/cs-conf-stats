STOC 2003 Acceptance Rate

STOC 2003 accepted 80 papers out of 270 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.6%.

ACM Symposium on Theory of Computing 2003 (35th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with STOC 2002, the acceptance rate decreased from 31.7% to 29.6%, and submissions decreased from 287 to 270.
