STOC 2013 Acceptance Rate

STOC 2013 accepted 100 papers out of 360 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.8%.

ACM Symposium on Theory of Computing 2013 (45th) was held in Palo Alto, California, USA.

Compared with STOC 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.7% to 27.8%, and submissions increased from 303 to 360.
