VLDB 2004 Acceptance Rate

VLDB 2004 accepted 83 papers out of 463 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.9%.

International Conference on Very Large Data Bases 2004 (30th) was held in Toronto, Canada.

Compared with VLDB 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 18.2% to 17.9%, and submissions increased from 411 to 463.
