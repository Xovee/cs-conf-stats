VLDB 2015 Acceptance Rate

VLDB 2015 accepted 151 papers out of 710 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.3%.

International Conference on Very Large Data Bases 2015 (41st) was held in Kohala Coast, Hawaii, USA.

Compared with VLDB 2013, the acceptance rate decreased from 22.7% to 21.3%, and submissions increased from 559 to 710.
