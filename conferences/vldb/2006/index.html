VLDB 2006 Acceptance Rate

VLDB 2006 accepted 83 papers out of 626 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 13.3%.

International Conference on Very Large Data Bases 2006 (32nd) was held in Seoul, Korea.

Compared with VLDB 2005, the acceptance rate decreased from 16.4% to 13.3%, and submissions increased from 517 to 626.
