VLDB 2013 Acceptance Rate

VLDB 2013 accepted 127 papers out of 559 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.7%.

International Conference on Very Large Data Bases 2013 (39th) was held in Trento, Italy.

Compared with VLDB 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 20.3% to 22.7%, and submissions decreased from 659 to 559.
