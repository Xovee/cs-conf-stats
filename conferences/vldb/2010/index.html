VLDB 2010 Acceptance Rate

VLDB 2010 accepted 84 papers out of 478 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.6%.

International Conference on Very Large Data Bases 2010 (36th) was held in Singapore, Singapore.

Compared with VLDB 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 17.4% to 17.6%, and submissions decreased from 575 to 478.
