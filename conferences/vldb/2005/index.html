VLDB 2005 Acceptance Rate

VLDB 2005 accepted 85 papers out of 517 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.4%.

International Conference on Very Large Data Bases 2005 (31st) was held in Trondheim, Norway.

Compared with VLDB 2004, the acceptance rate decreased from 17.9% to 16.4%, and submissions increased from 463 to 517.
