CHI 1991 Acceptance Rate

CHI 1991 accepted 56 papers out of 240 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.3%.

ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems 1991 (9th) was held in New Orleans, USA.

Compared with CHI 1990, the acceptance rate increased from 18.1% to 23.3%, and submissions decreased from 260 to 240.
