CHI 2004 Acceptance Rate

CHI 2004 accepted 93 papers out of 578 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.1%.

ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems 2004 (22nd) was held in Vienna, Austria.

Compared with CHI 2003, the acceptance rate increased from 16.0% to 16.1%, and submissions increased from 468 to 578.
