CHI 2017 Acceptance Rate

CHI 2017 accepted 600 papers out of 2,400 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.0%.

ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems 2017 (35th) was held in Denver, USA.

Compared with CHI 2016, the acceptance rate increased from 23.2% to 25.0%, and submissions decreased from 2,435 to 2,400.
