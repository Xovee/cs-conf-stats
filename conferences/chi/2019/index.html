CHI 2019 Acceptance Rate

CHI 2019 accepted 703 papers out of 2,958 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.8%.

ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems 2019 (37th) was held in Glasgow, Scotland, UK.

Compared with CHI 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 25.7% to 23.8%, and submissions increased from 2,592 to 2,958.
