CHI 2013 Acceptance Rate

CHI 2013 accepted 392 papers out of 1,963 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 20.0%.

ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems 2013 (31st) was held in Paris, France.

Compared with CHI 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.5% to 20.0%, and submissions increased from 1,577 to 1,963.
