CHI 2020 Acceptance Rate

CHI 2020 accepted 760 papers out of 3,126 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.3%.

ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems 2020 (38th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with CHI 2019, the acceptance rate increased from 23.8% to 24.3%, and submissions increased from 2,958 to 3,126.
