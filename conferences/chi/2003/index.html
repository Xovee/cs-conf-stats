CHI 2003 Acceptance Rate

CHI 2003 accepted 75 papers out of 468 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.0%.

ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems 2003 (21st) was held in Fort Lauderdale, USA.

Compared with CHI 2002, the acceptance rate increased from 14.7% to 16.0%, and submissions increased from 414 to 468.
