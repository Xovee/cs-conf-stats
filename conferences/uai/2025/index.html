UAI 2025 Acceptance Rate

UAI 2025 accepted 230 papers out of 750 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.7%.

Compared with UAI 2024, the acceptance rate increased from 28.0% to 30.7%, and submissions increased from 715 to 750.
