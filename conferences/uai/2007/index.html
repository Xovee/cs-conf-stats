UAI 2007 Acceptance Rate

UAI 2007 accepted 57 papers out of 180 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 31.7%.

Conference on Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence 2007 (23rd) was held in Vancouver, Canada.

Compared with UAI 2006, the acceptance rate increased from 31.6% to 31.7%, and submissions decreased from 215 to 180.
