UAI 2017 Acceptance Rate

UAI 2017 accepted 87 papers out of 282 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.9%.

Conference on Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence 2017 (33rd) was held in Sydney, Australia.

Compared with UAI 2016, the acceptance rate decreased from 30.9% to 30.9%, and submissions increased from 275 to 282.
