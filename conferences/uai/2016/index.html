UAI 2016 accepted 85 papers out of 275 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.9%.

Conference on Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence 2016 (32nd) was held in New York City, USA.

Compared with UAI 2015, the acceptance rate decreased from 34.0% to 30.9%, and submissions decreased from 291 to 275.
