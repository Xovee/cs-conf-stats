UAI 2022 Acceptance Rate

UAI 2022 accepted 230 papers out of 712 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 32.3%.

Conference on Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence 2022 (38th) was held in Eindhoven, The Netherlands.

Compared with UAI 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 29.5% to 32.3%, and submissions increased from 694 to 712.
