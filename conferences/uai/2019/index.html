UAI 2019 Acceptance Rate

UAI 2019 accepted 118 papers out of 450 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.2%.

Conference on Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence 2019 (35th) was held in Tel Aviv, Israel.

Compared with UAI 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 30.9% to 26.2%, and submissions increased from 337 to 450.
