AISTATS 2015 Acceptance Rate

AISTATS 2015 accepted 127 papers out of 442 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.7%.

International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics 2015 (18th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with AISTATS 2014, the acceptance rate decreased from 35.8% to 28.7%, and submissions increased from 335 to 442.
