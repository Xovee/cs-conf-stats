AISTATS 2007 Acceptance Rate

AISTATS 2007 accepted 85 papers out of 150 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 56.7%.

International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics 2007 (11th) was held in San Juan, Puerto Rico.

Compared with AISTATS 2005, the acceptance rate increased from 38.0% to 56.7%, and submissions increased from 150 to 150.
