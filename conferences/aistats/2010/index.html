AISTATS 2010 Acceptance Rate

AISTATS 2010 accepted 125 papers out of 304 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 41.1%.

International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics 2010 (13th) was held in Sardinia, Italy.

Compared with AISTATS 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 40.0% to 41.1%, and submissions increased from 210 to 304.
