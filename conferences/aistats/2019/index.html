AISTATS 2019 Acceptance Rate

AISTATS 2019 accepted 360 papers out of 1,111 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 32.4%.

International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics 2019 (22nd) was held in Naha, Japan.

Compared with AISTATS 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 33.2% to 32.4%, and submissions increased from 645 to 1,111.
