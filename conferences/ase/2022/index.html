ASE 2022 Acceptance Rate

ASE 2022 accepted 116 papers out of 527 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.0%.

ACM/IEEE International Conference on Automated Software Engineering 2022 (37th) was held in Oakland Center, Michigan, USA.

Compared with ASE 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 19.2% to 22.0%, and submissions increased from 427 to 527.
