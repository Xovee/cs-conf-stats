ASE 2003 Acceptance Rate

ASE 2003 accepted 22 papers out of 170 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 12.9%.

ACM/IEEE International Conference on Automated Software Engineering 2003 (18th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with ASE 2002, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.2% to 12.9%, and submissions increased from 94 to 170.
