ASE 2010 Acceptance Rate

ASE 2010 accepted 33 papers out of 190 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.4%.

ACM/IEEE International Conference on Automated Software Engineering 2010 (25th) was held in Antwerp, Belgium.

Compared with ASE 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 17.3% to 17.4%, and submissions decreased from 220 to 190.
