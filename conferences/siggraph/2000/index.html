SIGGRAPH 2000 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH 2000 accepted 59 papers out of 304 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.4%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference (International Conference on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques) 2000 (27th) was held in New Orleans, USA.

Compared with SIGGRAPH 1999, the acceptance rate increased from 16.3% to 19.4%, and submissions decreased from 320 to 304.
