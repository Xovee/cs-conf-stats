SIGGRAPH 2013 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH 2013 accepted 115 papers out of 480 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.0%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference (International Conference on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques) 2013 (40th) was held in Anaheim, California, USA.

Compared with SIGGRAPH 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 22.8% to 24.0%, and submissions increased from 412 to 480.
