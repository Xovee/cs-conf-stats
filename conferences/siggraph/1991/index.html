SIGGRAPH 1991 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH 1991 accepted 40 papers out of 207 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.3%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference (International Conference on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques) 1991 (18th) was held in Las Vegas, USA.

Compared with SIGGRAPH 1990, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.5% to 19.3%, and submissions decreased from 210 to 207.
