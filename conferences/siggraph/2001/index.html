SIGGRAPH 2001 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH 2001 accepted 65 papers out of 300 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.7%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference (International Conference on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques) 2001 (28th) was held in Los Angeles, California, USA.

Compared with SIGGRAPH 2000, the acceptance rate increased from 19.4% to 21.7%, and submissions decreased from 304 to 300.
