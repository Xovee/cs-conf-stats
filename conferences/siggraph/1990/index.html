SIGGRAPH 1990 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH 1990 accepted 43 papers out of 210 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 20.5%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference (International Conference on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques) 1990 (17th) was held in Dallas, USA.

Compared with SIGGRAPH 1989, the acceptance rate increased from 20.0% to 20.5%, and submissions increased from 190 to 210.
