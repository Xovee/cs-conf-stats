SIGGRAPH 2007 Acceptance Rate

SIGGRAPH 2007 accepted 108 papers out of 455 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.7%.

ACM SIGGRAPH Conference (International Conference on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques) 2007 (34th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with SIGGRAPH 2006, the acceptance rate increased from 18.1% to 23.7%, and submissions decreased from 474 to 455.
