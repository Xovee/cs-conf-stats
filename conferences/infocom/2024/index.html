INFOCOM 2024 Acceptance Rate

INFOCOM 2024 accepted 256 papers out of 1,307 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.6%.

IEEE International Conference on Computer Communications 2024 (43rd) was held in Vancouver, Canada.

Compared with INFOCOM 2023, the acceptance rate increased from 19.2% to 19.6%, and submissions decreased from 1,312 to 1,307.
