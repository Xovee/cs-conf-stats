INFOCOM 2001 Acceptance Rate

INFOCOM 2001 accepted 192 papers out of 826 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.2%.

IEEE International Conference on Computer Communications 2001 (20th) was held in Anchorage, USA.

Compared with INFOCOM 2000, the acceptance rate decreased from 26.8% to 23.2%, and submissions increased from 717 to 826.
