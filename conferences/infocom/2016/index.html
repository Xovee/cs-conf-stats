INFOCOM 2016 Acceptance Rate

INFOCOM 2016 accepted 300 papers out of 1,644 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.2%.

IEEE International Conference on Computer Communications 2016 (35th) was held in San Francisco, California, USA.

Compared with INFOCOM 2015, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.3% to 18.2%, and submissions increased from 1,640 to 1,644.
