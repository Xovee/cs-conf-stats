NAACL 2018 Acceptance Rate

NAACL 2018 accepted 331 papers out of 1,089 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.4%.

Annual Conference of the North American Chapter of the Association for Computational Linguistics. 2018 (13th) was held in New Orleans, USA.

Compared with NAACL 2016, the acceptance rate increased from 26.8% to 30.4%, and submissions increased from 680 to 1,089.
