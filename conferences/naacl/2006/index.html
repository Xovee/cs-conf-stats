NAACL 2006 Acceptance Rate

NAACL 2006 accepted 114 papers out of 384 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.7%.

Annual Conference of the North American Chapter of the Association for Computational Linguistics. 2006 (5th) was held in New York City, USA.

Compared with NAACL 2004, the acceptance rate decreased from 32.9% to 29.7%, and submissions increased from 252 to 384.
