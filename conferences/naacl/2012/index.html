NAACL 2012 Acceptance Rate

NAACL 2012 accepted 97 papers out of 301 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 32.2%.

Annual Conference of the North American Chapter of the Association for Computational Linguistics. 2012 (9th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with NAACL 2010, the acceptance rate decreased from 32.4% to 32.2%, and submissions decreased from 450 to 301.
