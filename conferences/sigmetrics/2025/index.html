SIGMETRICS 2025 Acceptance Rate

SIGMETRICS 2025 accepted 66 papers out of 382 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.3%.

ACM SIGMETRICS International Conference on Measurement and Modeling of Computer Systems 2025 (51st) was held in Stony Brook, USA.

Compared with SIGMETRICS 2024, the acceptance rate increased from 15.5% to 17.3%, and submissions increased from 336 to 382.
