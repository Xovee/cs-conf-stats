SIGMETRICS 1998 Acceptance Rate

SIGMETRICS 1998 accepted 25 papers out of 136 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.4%.

ACM SIGMETRICS International Conference on Measurement and Modeling of Computer Systems 1998 (24th) was held in Madison, USA.

Compared with SIGMETRICS 1997, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.2% to 18.4%, and submissions increased from 130 to 136.
