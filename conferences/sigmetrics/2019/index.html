SIGMETRICS 2019 Acceptance Rate

SIGMETRICS 2019 accepted 50 papers out of 317 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.8%.

ACM SIGMETRICS International Conference on Measurement and Modeling of Computer Systems 2019 (45th) was held in Phoenix, USA.

Compared with SIGMETRICS 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.0% to 15.8%, and submissions increased from 270 to 317.
