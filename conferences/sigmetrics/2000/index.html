SIGMETRICS 2000 Acceptance Rate

SIGMETRICS 2000 accepted 28 papers out of 165 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.0%.

ACM SIGMETRICS International Conference on Measurement and Modeling of Computer Systems 2000 (26th) was held in Santa Clara, California, USA.

Compared with SIGMETRICS 1999, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.6% to 17.0%, and submissions increased from 92 to 165.
