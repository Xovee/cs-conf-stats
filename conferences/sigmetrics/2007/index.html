SIGMETRICS 2007 Acceptance Rate

SIGMETRICS 2007 accepted 29 papers out of 179 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.2%.

ACM SIGMETRICS International Conference on Measurement and Modeling of Computer Systems 2007 (33rd) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with SIGMETRICS 2006, the acceptance rate increased from 13.7% to 16.2%, and submissions decreased from 219 to 179.
