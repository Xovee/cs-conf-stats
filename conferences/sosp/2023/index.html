SOSP 2023 Acceptance Rate

SOSP 2023 accepted 43 papers out of 232 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.5%.

Symposium on Operating Systems Principles 2023 (29th) was held in Koblenz, Germany.

Compared with SOSP 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 15.5% to 18.5%, and submissions decreased from 348 to 232.
