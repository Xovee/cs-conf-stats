IEEE S&P 2022 Acceptance Rate

IEEE S&P 2022 accepted 147 papers out of 1,012 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 14.5%.

IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy 2022 (43rd) was held in San Francisco, California, USA.

Compared with IEEE S&P 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 12.1% to 14.5%, and submissions increased from 952 to 1,012.
