HPCA 2010 Acceptance Rate

HPCA 2010 accepted 32 papers out of 175 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.3%.

IEEE International Symposium on High-Performance Computer Architecture 2010 (16th) was held in Bangalore, India.

Compared with HPCA 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.0% to 18.3%, and submissions decreased from 184 to 175.
