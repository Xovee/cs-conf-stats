HPCA 2011 Acceptance Rate

HPCA 2011 accepted 42 papers out of 227 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.5%.

IEEE International Symposium on High-Performance Computer Architecture 2011 (17th) was held in San Antonio, USA.

Compared with HPCA 2010, the acceptance rate increased from 18.3% to 18.5%, and submissions increased from 175 to 227.
