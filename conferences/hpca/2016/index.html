HPCA 2016 Acceptance Rate

HPCA 2016 accepted 53 papers out of 240 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.1%.

IEEE International Symposium on High-Performance Computer Architecture 2016 (22nd) was held in Barcelona, Spain.

Compared with HPCA 2015, the acceptance rate decreased from 22.4% to 22.1%, and submissions increased from 228 to 240.
