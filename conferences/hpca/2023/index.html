HPCA 2023 Acceptance Rate

HPCA 2023 accepted 91 papers out of 364 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.0%.

IEEE International Symposium on High-Performance Computer Architecture 2023 (29th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with HPCA 2022, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.3% to 25.0%, and submissions increased from 273 to 364.
