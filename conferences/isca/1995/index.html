ISCA 1995 Acceptance Rate

ISCA 1995 accepted 37 papers out of 180 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 20.6%.

International Symposium on Computer Architecture 1995 (22nd) was held in Santa Margherita Ligure, Italy.

Compared with ISCA 1994, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.8% to 20.6%, and submissions increased from 143 to 180.
