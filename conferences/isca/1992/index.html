ISCA 1992 Acceptance Rate

ISCA 1992 accepted 39 papers out of 173 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.5%.

International Symposium on Computer Architecture 1992 (19th) was held in Queensland, Australia.

Compared with ISCA 1991, the acceptance rate increased from 19.3% to 22.5%, and submissions decreased from 197 to 173.
