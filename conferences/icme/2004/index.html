ICME 2004 Acceptance Rate

ICME 2004 accepted 579 papers out of 841 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 68.8%.

IEEE International Conference on Multimedia and Expo 2004 (5th) was held in Taipei, Taiwan, China.

Compared with ICME 2000, the acceptance rate increased from 61.5% to 68.8%, and submissions increased from 650 to 841.
