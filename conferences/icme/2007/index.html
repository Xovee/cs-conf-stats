ICME 2007 Acceptance Rate

ICME 2007 accepted 573 papers out of 1,200 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 47.8%.

IEEE International Conference on Multimedia and Expo 2007 (8th) was held in Beijing, China.

Compared with ICME 2006, the acceptance rate decreased from 54.0% to 47.8%, and submissions increased from 1,022 to 1,200.
