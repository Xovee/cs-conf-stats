ICME 2014 Acceptance Rate

ICME 2014 accepted 212 papers out of 716 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.6%.

IEEE International Conference on Multimedia and Expo 2014 (15th) was held in Chengdu, China.

Compared with ICME 2013, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.9% to 29.6%, and submissions increased from 622 to 716.
