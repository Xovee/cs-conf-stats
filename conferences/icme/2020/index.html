ICME 2020 Acceptance Rate

ICME 2020 accepted 241 papers out of 834 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.9%.

IEEE International Conference on Multimedia and Expo 2020 (21st) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with ICME 2019, the acceptance rate decreased from 31.2% to 28.9%, and submissions decreased from 1,002 to 834.
