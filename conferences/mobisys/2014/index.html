MobiSys 2014 Acceptance Rate

MobiSys 2014 accepted 25 papers out of 185 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 13.5%.

ACM International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services 2014 (12th) was held in Bretton Woods, USA.

Compared with MobiSys 2013, the acceptance rate decreased from 15.6% to 13.5%, and submissions decreased from 211 to 185.
