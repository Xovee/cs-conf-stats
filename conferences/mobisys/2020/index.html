MobiSys 2020 Acceptance Rate

MobiSys 2020 accepted 34 papers out of 175 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.4%.

ACM International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services 2020 (18th) was held in Toronto, Canada.

Compared with MobiSys 2019, the acceptance rate decreased from 22.7% to 19.4%, and submissions increased from 172 to 175.
