MobiSys 2018 Acceptance Rate

MobiSys 2018 accepted 37 papers out of 138 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.8%.

ACM International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services 2018 (16th) was held in Munich, Germany.

Compared with MobiSys 2017, the acceptance rate increased from 18.1% to 26.8%, and submissions decreased from 188 to 138.
