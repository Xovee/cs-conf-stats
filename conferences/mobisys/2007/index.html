MobiSys 2007 Acceptance Rate

MobiSys 2007 accepted 22 papers out of 105 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.0%.

Compared with MobiSys 2006, the acceptance rate increased from 15.4% to 21.0%, and submissions decreased from 123 to 105.
