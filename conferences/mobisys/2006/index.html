MobiSys 2006 Acceptance Rate

MobiSys 2006 accepted 19 papers out of 123 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.4%.

ACM International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services 2006 (4th) was held in Uppsala, Sweden.

Compared with MobiSys 2005, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.8% to 15.4%, and submissions increased from 84 to 123.
