MobiSys 2010 Acceptance Rate

MobiSys 2010 accepted 25 papers out of 126 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.8%.

ACM International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services 2010 (8th) was held in San Francisco, California, USA.

Compared with MobiSys 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.3% to 19.8%, and submissions decreased from 128 to 126.
