POPL 2010 Acceptance Rate

POPL 2010 accepted 39 papers out of 207 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.8%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages 2010 (37th) was held in Madrid, Spain.

Compared with POPL 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 22.5% to 18.8%, and submissions increased from 160 to 207.
