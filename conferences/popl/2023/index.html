POPL 2023 Acceptance Rate

POPL 2023 accepted 74 papers out of 275 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.9%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages 2023 (50th) was held in Boston, USA.

Compared with POPL 2022, the acceptance rate increased from 22.7% to 26.9%, and submissions decreased from 286 to 275.
