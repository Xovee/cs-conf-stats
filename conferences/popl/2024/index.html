POPL 2024 Acceptance Rate

POPL 2024 accepted 93 papers out of 314 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.6%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages 2024 (51st) was held in London, UK.

Compared with POPL 2023, the acceptance rate increased from 26.9% to 29.6%, and submissions increased from 275 to 314.
