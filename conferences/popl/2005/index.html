POPL 2005 Acceptance Rate

POPL 2005 accepted 31 papers out of 170 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.2%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages 2005 (32nd) was held in Long Beach, California, USA.

Compared with POPL 2004, the acceptance rate increased from 16.5% to 18.2%, and submissions decreased from 176 to 170.
