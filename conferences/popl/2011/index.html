POPL 2011 Acceptance Rate

POPL 2011 accepted 49 papers out of 209 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.4%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages 2011 (38th) was held in Austin, USA.

Compared with POPL 2010, the acceptance rate increased from 18.8% to 23.4%, and submissions increased from 207 to 209.
