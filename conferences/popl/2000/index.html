POPL 2000 Acceptance Rate

POPL 2000 accepted 30 papers out of 151 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.9%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages 2000 (27th) was held in Boston, USA.

Compared with POPL 1999, the acceptance rate increased from 17.6% to 19.9%, and submissions increased from 136 to 151.
