POPL 1986 Acceptance Rate

POPL 1986 accepted 30 papers out of 165 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.2%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages 1986 (13th) was held in St. Petersburg, USA.

Compared with POPL 1983, the acceptance rate increased from 16.5% to 18.2%, and submissions decreased from 170 to 165.
