POPL 2019 Acceptance Rate

POPL 2019 accepted 77 papers out of 267 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.8%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages 2019 (46th) was held in Cascais, Portugal.

Compared with POPL 2018, the acceptance rate increased from 24.4% to 28.8%, and submissions decreased from 271 to 267.
