POPL 2009 Acceptance Rate

POPL 2009 accepted 36 papers out of 160 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.5%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages 2009 (36th) was held in Savannah, USA.

Compared with POPL 2008, the acceptance rate increased from 16.5% to 22.5%, and submissions decreased from 212 to 160.
