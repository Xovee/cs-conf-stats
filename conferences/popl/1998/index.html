POPL 1998 Acceptance Rate

POPL 1998 accepted 32 papers out of 175 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.3%.

Compared with POPL 1997, the acceptance rate increased from 16.0% to 18.3%, and submissions decreased from 225 to 175.
