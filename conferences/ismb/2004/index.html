ISMB 2004 Acceptance Rate

ISMB 2004 accepted 67 papers out of 492 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 13.6%.

Intelligent Systems for Molecular Biology 2004 (12th) was held in Glasgow, Scotland, UK.

Compared with ISMB 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 14.1% to 13.6%, and submissions increased from 341 to 492.
