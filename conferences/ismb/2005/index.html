ISMB 2005 Acceptance Rate

ISMB 2005 accepted 56 papers out of 428 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 13.1%.

Intelligent Systems for Molecular Biology 2005 (13th) was held in Detroit, USA.

Compared with ISMB 2004, the acceptance rate decreased from 13.6% to 13.1%, and submissions decreased from 492 to 428.
