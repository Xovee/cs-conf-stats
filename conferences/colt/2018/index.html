COLT 2018 Acceptance Rate

COLT 2018 accepted 91 papers out of 335 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.2%.

Annual Conference on Computational Learning Theory. 2018 (31st) was held in Stockholm, Sweden.

Compared with COLT 2017, the acceptance rate decreased from 32.0% to 27.2%, and submissions increased from 228 to 335.
