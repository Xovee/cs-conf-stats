HPDC 2006 Acceptance Rate

HPDC 2006 accepted 24 papers out of 157 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.3%.

International Symposium on High-Performance Parallel Distributed Computing 2006 (15th) was held in Paris, France.

Compared with HPDC 2005, the acceptance rate decreased from 17.9% to 15.3%, and submissions increased from 140 to 157.
