HPDC 2013 Acceptance Rate

HPDC 2013 accepted 20 papers out of 131 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.3%.

International Symposium on High-Performance Parallel Distributed Computing 2013 (22nd) was held in New York City, USA.

Compared with HPDC 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 16.1% to 15.3%, and submissions decreased from 143 to 131.
