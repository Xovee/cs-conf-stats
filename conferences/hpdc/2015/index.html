HPDC 2015 Acceptance Rate

HPDC 2015 accepted 19 papers out of 116 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.4%.

International Symposium on High-Performance Parallel Distributed Computing 2015 (24th) was held in Portland, USA.

Compared with HPDC 2014, the acceptance rate increased from 16.2% to 16.4%, and submissions decreased from 130 to 116.
