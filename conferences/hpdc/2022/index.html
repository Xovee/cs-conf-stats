HPDC 2022 Acceptance Rate

HPDC 2022 accepted 21 papers out of 108 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.4%.

International Symposium on High-Performance Parallel Distributed Computing 2022 (31st) was held in Minneapolis, USA.

Compared with HPDC 2021, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.7% to 19.4%, and submissions increased from 92 to 108.
