UIST 2004 Acceptance Rate

UIST 2004 accepted 36 papers out of 170 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.2%.

ACM Symposium on User Interface Software and Technology 2004 (17th) was held in Santa Fe, USA.

Compared with UIST 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 21.6% to 21.2%, and submissions increased from 116 to 170.
