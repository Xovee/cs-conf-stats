UIST 2010 Acceptance Rate

UIST 2010 accepted 38 papers out of 207 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.4%.

ACM Symposium on User Interface Software and Technology 2010 (23rd) was held in New York City, USA.

Compared with UIST 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 18.0% to 18.4%, and submissions increased from 183 to 207.
