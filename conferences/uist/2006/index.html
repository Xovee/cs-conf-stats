UIST 2006 Acceptance Rate

UIST 2006 accepted 40 papers out of 177 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.6%.

ACM Symposium on User Interface Software and Technology 2006 (19th) was held in Montreux, Switzerland.

Compared with UIST 2005, the acceptance rate increased from 19.5% to 22.6%, and submissions increased from 159 to 177.
