UIST 2025 Acceptance Rate

UIST 2025 accepted 210 papers out of 947 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.2%.

ACM Symposium on User Interface Software and Technology 2025 (38th) was held in Busan, Korea.

Compared with UIST 2024, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.0% to 22.2%, and submissions increased from 608 to 947.
