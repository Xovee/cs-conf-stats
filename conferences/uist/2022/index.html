UIST 2022 Acceptance Rate

UIST 2022 accepted 98 papers out of 372 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.3%.

ACM Symposium on User Interface Software and Technology 2022 (35th) was held in Bend, USA.

Compared with UIST 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 25.9% to 26.3%, and submissions increased from 367 to 372.
