UIST 2017 Acceptance Rate

UIST 2017 accepted 73 papers out of 324 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.5%.

ACM Symposium on User Interface Software and Technology 2017 (30th) was held in Quebec City, Canada.

Compared with UIST 2016, the acceptance rate increased from 20.6% to 22.5%, and submissions decreased from 384 to 324.
