UIST 2008 Acceptance Rate

UIST 2008 accepted 25 papers out of 115 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.7%.

ACM Symposium on User Interface Software and Technology 2008 (21st) was held in Monterey, California, USA.

Compared with UIST 2007, the acceptance rate increased from 18.6% to 21.7%, and submissions decreased from 129 to 115.
