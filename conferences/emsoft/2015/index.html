EMSOFT 2015 Acceptance Rate

EMSOFT 2015 accepted 22 papers out of 85 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.9%.

International Conference on Embedded Software 2015 (15th) was held in Amsterdam, The Netherlands.

Compared with EMSOFT 2014, the acceptance rate increased from 24.8% to 25.9%, and submissions decreased from 117 to 85.
