EMSOFT 2018 Acceptance Rate

EMSOFT 2018 accepted 27 papers out of 111 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.3%.

International Conference on Embedded Software 2018 (18th) was held in Turin, Italy.

Compared with EMSOFT 2016, the acceptance rate decreased from 25.8% to 24.3%, and submissions increased from 97 to 111.
