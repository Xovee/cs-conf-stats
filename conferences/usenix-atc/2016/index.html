USENIX ATC 2016 Acceptance Rate

USENIX ATC 2016 accepted 46 papers out of 213 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.6%.

USENIX Annual Technical Conference 2016 (45th) was held in Denver, USA.

Compared with USENIX ATC 2015, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.8% to 21.6%, and submissions increased from 189 to 213.
