NSDI 2022 Acceptance Rate

NSDI 2022 accepted 78 papers out of 396 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.7%.

USENIX Symposium on Networked Systems Design and Implementation 2022 (19th) was held in Renton, USA.

Compared with NSDI 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 16.0% to 19.7%, and submissions increased from 369 to 396.
