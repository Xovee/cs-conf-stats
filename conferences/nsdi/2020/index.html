NSDI 2020 Acceptance Rate

NSDI 2020 accepted 65 papers out of 354 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.4%.

USENIX Symposium on Networked Systems Design and Implementation 2020 (17th) was held in Santa Clara, California, USA.

Compared with NSDI 2019, the acceptance rate increased from 14.8% to 18.4%, and submissions increased from 332 to 354.
