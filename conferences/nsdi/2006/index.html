NSDI 2006 Acceptance Rate

NSDI 2006 accepted 28 papers out of 110 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.5%.

USENIX Symposium on Networked Systems Design and Implementation 2006 (3rd) was held in San Jose, California, USA.

Compared with NSDI 2005, the acceptance rate increased from 22.3% to 25.5%, and submissions decreased from 112 to 110.
