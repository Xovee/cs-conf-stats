NSDI 2007 Acceptance Rate

NSDI 2007 accepted 27 papers out of 113 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.9%.

USENIX Symposium on Networked Systems Design and Implementation 2007 (4th) was held in Cambridge (MA), USA.

Compared with NSDI 2006, the acceptance rate decreased from 25.5% to 23.9%, and submissions increased from 110 to 113.
