NSDI 2013 Acceptance Rate

NSDI 2013 accepted 38 papers out of 170 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.4%.

USENIX Symposium on Networked Systems Design and Implementation 2013 (10th) was held in Lombard, USA.

Compared with NSDI 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 17.8% to 22.4%, and submissions increased from 169 to 170.
