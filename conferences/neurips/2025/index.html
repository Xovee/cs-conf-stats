NeurIPS 2025 Acceptance Rate

NeurIPS 2025 accepted 5,290 papers out of 21,575 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.5%.

Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems 2025 (39th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with NeurIPS 2024, the acceptance rate decreased from 25.8% to 24.5%, and submissions increased from 15,671 to 21,575.
