NeurIPS 2015 Acceptance Rate

NeurIPS 2015 accepted 403 papers out of 1,838 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.9%.

Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems 2015 (29th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with NeurIPS 2014, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.7% to 21.9%, and submissions increased from 1,678 to 1,838.
