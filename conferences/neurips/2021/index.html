NeurIPS 2021 Acceptance Rate

NeurIPS 2021 accepted 2,334 papers out of 9,122 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.6%.

Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems 2021 (35th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with NeurIPS 2020, the acceptance rate increased from 20.0% to 25.6%, and submissions decreased from 9,467 to 9,122.
