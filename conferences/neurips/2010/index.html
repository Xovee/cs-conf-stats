NeurIPS 2010 Acceptance Rate

NeurIPS 2010 accepted 292 papers out of 1,219 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.0%.

Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems 2010 (24th) was held in Vancouver, Canada.

Compared with NeurIPS 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 23.8% to 24.0%, and submissions increased from 1,105 to 1,219.
