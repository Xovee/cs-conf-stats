NeurIPS 2011 Acceptance Rate

NeurIPS 2011 accepted 306 papers out of 1,400 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.9%.

Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems 2011 (25th) was held in Granada, Spain.

Compared with NeurIPS 2010, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.0% to 21.9%, and submissions increased from 1,219 to 1,400.
