NeurIPS 2007 Acceptance Rate

NeurIPS 2007 accepted 217 papers out of 975 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.3%.

Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems 2007 (21st) was held in Vancouver, Canada.

Compared with NeurIPS 2006, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.5% to 22.3%, and submissions increased from 833 to 975.
