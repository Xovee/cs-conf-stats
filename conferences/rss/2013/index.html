RSS 2013 Acceptance Rate

RSS 2013 accepted 55 papers out of 193 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.5%.

Robotics: Science and Systems 2013 (9th) was held in Berlin, Germany.

Compared with RSS 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 33.9% to 28.5%, and submissions increased from 177 to 193.
