RSS 2012 Acceptance Rate

RSS 2012 accepted 60 papers out of 177 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 33.9%.

Robotics: Science and Systems 2012 (8th) was held in Sydney, Australia.

Compared with RSS 2011, the acceptance rate increased from 24.6% to 33.9%, and submissions decreased from 183 to 177.
