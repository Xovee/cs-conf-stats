ICLR 2021 Acceptance Rate

ICLR 2021 accepted 860 papers out of 2,997 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.7%.

International Conference on Learning Representations 2021 (9th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with ICLR 2020, the acceptance rate increased from 26.5% to 28.7%, and submissions increased from 2,594 to 2,997.
