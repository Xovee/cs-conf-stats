ICLR 2025 Acceptance Rate

ICLR 2025 accepted 3,710 papers out of 11,565 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 32.1%.

International Conference on Learning Representations 2025 (13th) was held in Singapore, Singapore.

Compared with ICLR 2024, the acceptance rate increased from 30.8% to 32.1%, and submissions increased from 7,304 to 11,565.
