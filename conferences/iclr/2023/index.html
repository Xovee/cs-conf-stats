ICLR 2023 Acceptance Rate

ICLR 2023 accepted 1,574 papers out of 4,938 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 31.9%.

International Conference on Learning Representations 2023 (11th) was held in Kigali, Rwanda.

Compared with ICLR 2022, the acceptance rate decreased from 32.3% to 31.9%, and submissions increased from 3,391 to 4,938.
