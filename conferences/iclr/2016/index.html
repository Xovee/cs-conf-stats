ICLR 2016 Acceptance Rate

ICLR 2016 accepted 80 papers out of 265 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.2%.

International Conference on Learning Representations 2016 (4th) was held in San Juan, Puerto Rico.

Compared with ICLR 2015, the acceptance rate increased from 21.7% to 30.2%, and submissions increased from 143 to 265.
