ICLR 2024 Acceptance Rate

ICLR 2024 accepted 2,250 papers out of 7,304 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.8%.

International Conference on Learning Representations 2024 (12th) was held in Vienna, Austria.

Compared with ICLR 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 31.9% to 30.8%, and submissions increased from 4,938 to 7,304.
