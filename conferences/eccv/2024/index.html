ECCV 2024 Acceptance Rate

ECCV 2024 accepted 2,387 papers out of 8,585 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.8%.

European Conference on Computer Vision 2024 (18th) was held in Milan, Italy.

Compared with ECCV 2022, the acceptance rate decreased from 28.3% to 27.8%, and submissions increased from 5,804 to 8,585.
