ECCV 2014 Acceptance Rate

ECCV 2014 accepted 363 papers out of 1,444 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.1%.

European Conference on Computer Vision 2014 (13th) was held in Zurich, Switzerland.

Compared with ECCV 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 28.4% to 25.1%, and submissions increased from 1,437 to 1,444.
