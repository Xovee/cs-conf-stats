ECCV 2012 Acceptance Rate

ECCV 2012 accepted 408 papers out of 1,437 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.4%.

European Conference on Computer Vision 2012 (12th) was held in Florence, Italy.

Compared with ECCV 2010, the acceptance rate increased from 27.7% to 28.4%, and submissions increased from 1,174 to 1,437.
