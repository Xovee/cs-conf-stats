ECCV 1998 Acceptance Rate

ECCV 1998 accepted 112 papers out of 223 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 50.2%.

European Conference on Computer Vision 1998 (5th) was held in Freiburg, Germany.

Compared with ECCV 1996, the acceptance rate increased from 37.5% to 50.2%, and submissions decreased from 328 to 223.
