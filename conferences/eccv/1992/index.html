ECCV 1992 Acceptance Rate

ECCV 1992 accepted 105 papers out of 308 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 34.1%.

European Conference on Computer Vision 1992 (2nd) was held in Santa Margherita Ligure, Italy.

Compared with ECCV 1990, the acceptance rate increased from 27.2% to 34.1%, and submissions increased from 298 to 308.
