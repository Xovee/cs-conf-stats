ECCV 2000 Acceptance Rate

ECCV 2000 accepted 116 papers out of 266 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 43.6%.

European Conference on Computer Vision 2000 (6th) was held in Dublin, Ireland.

Compared with ECCV 1998, the acceptance rate decreased from 50.2% to 43.6%, and submissions increased from 223 to 266.
