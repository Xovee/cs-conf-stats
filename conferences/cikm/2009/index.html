CIKM 2009 Acceptance Rate

CIKM 2009 accepted 123 papers out of 847 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 14.5%.

ACM International Conference on Information and Knowledge Management 2009 (18th) was held in Beijing, China.

Compared with CIKM 2008, the acceptance rate decreased from 16.5% to 14.5%, and submissions increased from 800 to 847.
