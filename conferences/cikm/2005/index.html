CIKM 2005 Acceptance Rate

CIKM 2005 accepted 77 papers out of 425 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.1%.

ACM International Conference on Information and Knowledge Management 2005 (14th) was held in Bremen, Germany.

Compared with CIKM 2004, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.8% to 18.1%, and submissions increased from 303 to 425.
