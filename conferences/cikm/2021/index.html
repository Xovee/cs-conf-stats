CIKM 2021 Acceptance Rate

CIKM 2021 accepted 271 papers out of 1,251 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.7%.

ACM International Conference on Information and Knowledge Management 2021 (30th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with CIKM 2020, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.3% to 21.7%, and submissions increased from 1,175 to 1,251.
