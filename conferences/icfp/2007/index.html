ICFP 2007 Acceptance Rate

ICFP 2007 accepted 32 papers out of 122 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.2%.

ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Functional Programming 2007 (12th) was held in Freiburg, Germany.

Compared with ICFP 2006, the acceptance rate decreased from 32.4% to 26.2%, and submissions increased from 74 to 122.
