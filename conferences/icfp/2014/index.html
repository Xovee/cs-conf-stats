ICFP 2014 Acceptance Rate

ICFP 2014 accepted 28 papers out of 97 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.9%.

ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Functional Programming 2014 (19th) was held in Gothenburg, Sweden.

Compared with ICFP 2013, the acceptance rate decreased from 30.1% to 28.9%, and submissions decreased from 133 to 97.
