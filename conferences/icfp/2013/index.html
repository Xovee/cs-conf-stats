ICFP 2013 Acceptance Rate

ICFP 2013 accepted 40 papers out of 133 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.1%.

ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Functional Programming 2013 (18th) was held in Boston, USA.

Compared with ICFP 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 36.4% to 30.1%, and submissions increased from 88 to 133.
