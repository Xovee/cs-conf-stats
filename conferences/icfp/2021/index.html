ICFP 2021 Acceptance Rate

ICFP 2021 accepted 35 papers out of 107 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 32.7%.

ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Functional Programming 2021 (26th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with ICFP 2020, the acceptance rate decreased from 36.6% to 32.7%, and submissions increased from 101 to 107.
