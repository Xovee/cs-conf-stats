ICFP 2003 Acceptance Rate

ICFP 2003 accepted 24 papers out of 95 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.3%.

ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Functional Programming 2003 (8th) was held in Uppsala, Sweden.

Compared with ICFP 2002, the acceptance rate decreased from 31.6% to 25.3%, and submissions increased from 76 to 95.
