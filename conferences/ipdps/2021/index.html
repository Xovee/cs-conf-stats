IPDPS 2021 Acceptance Rate

IPDPS 2021 accepted 105 papers out of 462 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.7%.

IEEE International Parallel and Distributed Processing Symposium 2021 (35th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with IPDPS 2020, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.7% to 22.7%, and submissions increased from 446 to 462.
