IPDPS 2000 Acceptance Rate

IPDPS 2000 accepted 107 papers out of 303 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 35.3%.

IEEE International Parallel and Distributed Processing Symposium 2000 (14th) was held in Cancun, Mexico.

Compared with IPDPS 1999, the acceptance rate decreased from 43.5% to 35.3%, and submissions increased from 260 to 303.
