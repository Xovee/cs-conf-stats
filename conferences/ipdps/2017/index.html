IPDPS 2017 Acceptance Rate

IPDPS 2017 accepted 116 papers out of 508 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.8%.

IEEE International Parallel and Distributed Processing Symposium 2017 (31st) was held in Orlando, USA.

Compared with IPDPS 2016, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.0% to 22.8%, and submissions increased from 496 to 508.
