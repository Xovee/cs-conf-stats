IPDPS 2019 Acceptance Rate

IPDPS 2019 accepted 103 papers out of 372 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.7%.

IEEE International Parallel and Distributed Processing Symposium 2019 (33rd) was held in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Compared with IPDPS 2018, the acceptance rate increased from 25.9% to 27.7%, and submissions decreased from 436 to 372.
