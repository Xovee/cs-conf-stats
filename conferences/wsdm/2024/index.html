WSDM 2024 Acceptance Rate

WSDM 2024 accepted 109 papers out of 615 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.7%.

ACM International Conference on Web Search and Data Mining 2024 (17th) was held in Merida, Mexico.

Compared with WSDM 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 17.8% to 17.7%, and submissions decreased from 690 to 615.
