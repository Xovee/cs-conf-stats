WSDM 2019 Acceptance Rate

WSDM 2019 accepted 84 papers out of 511 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.4%.

ACM International Conference on Web Search and Data Mining 2019 (12th) was held in Melbourne, Australia.

Compared with WSDM 2018, the acceptance rate increased from 15.8% to 16.4%, and submissions decreased from 514 to 511.
