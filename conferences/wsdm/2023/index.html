WSDM 2023 Acceptance Rate

WSDM 2023 accepted 123 papers out of 690 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.8%.

ACM International Conference on Web Search and Data Mining 2023 (16th) was held in Singapore, Singapore.

Compared with WSDM 2022, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.3% to 17.8%, and submissions decreased from 790 to 690.
