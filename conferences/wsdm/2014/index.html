WSDM 2014 Acceptance Rate

WSDM 2014 accepted 64 papers out of 355 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.0%.

ACM International Conference on Web Search and Data Mining 2014 (7th) was held in New York City, USA.

Compared with WSDM 2013, the acceptance rate decreased from 18.9% to 18.0%, and submissions decreased from 387 to 355.
