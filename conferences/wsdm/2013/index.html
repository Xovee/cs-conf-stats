WSDM 2013 Acceptance Rate

WSDM 2013 accepted 73 papers out of 387 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.9%.

ACM International Conference on Web Search and Data Mining 2013 (6th) was held in Rome, Italy.

Compared with WSDM 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.7% to 18.9%, and submissions increased from 363 to 387.
