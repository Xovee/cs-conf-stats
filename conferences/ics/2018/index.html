ICS 2018 Acceptance Rate

ICS 2018 accepted 36 papers out of 193 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.7%.

International Conference on Supercomputing 2018 (32nd) was held in Beijing, China.

Compared with ICS 2017, the acceptance rate increased from 15.8% to 18.7%, and submissions increased from 177 to 193.
