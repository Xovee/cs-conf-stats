ICS 2001 accepted 45 papers out of 133 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 33.8%.

International Conference on Supercomputing 2001 (15th) was held in Sorrento, Italy.

Compared with ICS 2000, the acceptance rate increased from 27.0% to 33.8%, and submissions increased from 122 to 133.
