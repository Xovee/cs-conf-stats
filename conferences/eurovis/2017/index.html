EuroVis 2017 Acceptance Rate

EuroVis 2017 accepted 46 papers out of 170 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.1%.

Eurographics Conference on Visualization 2017 (19th) was held in Barcelona, Spain.

Compared with EuroVis 2016, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.3% to 27.1%, and submissions decreased from 183 to 170.
