EuroVis 2010 Acceptance Rate

EuroVis 2010 accepted 48 papers out of 164 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.3%.

Eurographics Conference on Visualization 2010 (12th) was held in Bordeaux, France.

Compared with EuroVis 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 28.7% to 29.3%, and submissions increased from 143 to 164.
