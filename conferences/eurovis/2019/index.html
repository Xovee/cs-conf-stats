EuroVis 2019 Acceptance Rate

EuroVis 2019 accepted 59 papers out of 189 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 31.2%.

Eurographics Conference on Visualization 2019 (21st) was held in Porto, Portugal.

Compared with EuroVis 2018, the acceptance rate increased from 29.0% to 31.2%, and submissions increased from 162 to 189.
