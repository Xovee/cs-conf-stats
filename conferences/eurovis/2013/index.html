EuroVis 2013 Acceptance Rate

EuroVis 2013 accepted 49 papers out of 177 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.7%.

Eurographics Conference on Visualization 2013 (15th) was held in Leipzig, Germany.

Compared with EuroVis 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 25.7% to 27.7%, and submissions decreased from 202 to 177.
