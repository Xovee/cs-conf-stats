EuroVis 2012 Acceptance Rate

EuroVis 2012 accepted 52 papers out of 202 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.7%.

Eurographics Conference on Visualization 2012 (14th) was held in Vienna, Austria.

Compared with EuroVis 2011, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.8% to 25.7%, and submissions increased from 194 to 202.
