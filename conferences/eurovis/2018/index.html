EuroVis 2018 Acceptance Rate

EuroVis 2018 accepted 47 papers out of 162 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.0%.

Eurographics Conference on Visualization 2018 (20th) was held in Brno, Czech Republic.

Compared with EuroVis 2017, the acceptance rate increased from 27.1% to 29.0%, and submissions decreased from 170 to 162.
