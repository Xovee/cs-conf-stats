EuroVis 2005 Acceptance Rate

EuroVis 2005 accepted 36 papers out of 102 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 35.3%.

Eurographics Conference on Visualization 2005 (7th) was held in Leeds, UK.

Compared with EuroVis 2004, the acceptance rate decreased from 47.6% to 35.3%, and submissions increased from 82 to 102.
