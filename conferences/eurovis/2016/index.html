EuroVis 2016 accepted 50 papers out of 183 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.3%.

Eurographics Conference on Visualization 2016 (18th) was held in Groningen, The Netherlands.

Compared with EuroVis 2015, the acceptance rate increased from 18.8% to 27.3%, and submissions increased from 160 to 183.
