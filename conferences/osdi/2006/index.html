OSDI 2006 Acceptance Rate

OSDI 2006 accepted 27 papers out of 155 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.4%.

USENIX Symposium on Operating Systems Design and Implementation 2006 (7th) was held in Seattle, USA.

Compared with OSDI 2004, the acceptance rate increased from 14.0% to 17.4%, and submissions decreased from 193 to 155.
