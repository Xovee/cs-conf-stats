OSDI 2020 Acceptance Rate

OSDI 2020 accepted 70 papers out of 400 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.5%.

USENIX Symposium on Operating Systems Design and Implementation 2020 (14th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with OSDI 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 18.3% to 17.5%, and submissions increased from 257 to 400.
