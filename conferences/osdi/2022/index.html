OSDI 2022 Acceptance Rate

OSDI 2022 accepted 54 papers out of 253 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.3%.

USENIX Symposium on Operating Systems Design and Implementation 2022 (16th) was held in Carlsbad, California, USA.

Compared with OSDI 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 18.8% to 21.3%, and submissions increased from 165 to 253.
