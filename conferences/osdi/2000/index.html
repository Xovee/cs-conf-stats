OSDI 2000 Acceptance Rate

OSDI 2000 accepted 24 papers out of 111 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.6%.

USENIX Symposium on Operating Systems Design and Implementation 2000 (4th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with OSDI 1999, the acceptance rate increased from 20.8% to 21.6%, and submissions increased from 96 to 111.
