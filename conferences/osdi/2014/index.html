OSDI 2014 Acceptance Rate

OSDI 2014 accepted 42 papers out of 228 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.4%.

USENIX Symposium on Operating Systems Design and Implementation 2014 (11th) was held in Broomfield, USA.

Compared with OSDI 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 11.6% to 18.4%, and submissions increased from 215 to 228.
