OSDI 2018 Acceptance Rate

OSDI 2018 accepted 47 papers out of 257 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.3%.

USENIX Symposium on Operating Systems Design and Implementation 2018 (13th) was held in Carlsbad, California, USA.

Compared with OSDI 2016, the acceptance rate increased from 18.1% to 18.3%, and submissions decreased from 260 to 257.
