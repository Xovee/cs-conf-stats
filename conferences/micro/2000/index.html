MICRO 2000 Acceptance Rate

MICRO 2000 accepted 31 papers out of 110 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.2%.

IEEE/ACM International Symposium on Microarchitecture 2000 (33rd) was held in Monterey, California, USA.

Compared with MICRO 1999, the acceptance rate increased from 20.6% to 28.2%, and submissions decreased from 131 to 110.
