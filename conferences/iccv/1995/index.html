ICCV 1995 Acceptance Rate

ICCV 1995 accepted 161 papers out of 600 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.8%.

International Conference on Computer Vision 1995 (5th) was held in Boston, USA.

Compared with ICCV 1993, the acceptance rate increased from 26.6% to 26.8%, and submissions increased from 380 to 600.
