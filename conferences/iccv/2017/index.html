ICCV 2017 Acceptance Rate

ICCV 2017 accepted 621 papers out of 2,143 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.0%.

International Conference on Computer Vision 2017 (16th) was held in Venice, Italy.

Compared with ICCV 2015, the acceptance rate decreased from 30.9% to 29.0%, and submissions increased from 1,698 to 2,143.
