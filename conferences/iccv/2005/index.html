ICCV 2005 Acceptance Rate

ICCV 2005 accepted 244 papers out of 1,230 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.8%.

International Conference on Computer Vision 2005 (10th) was held in Beijing, China.

Compared with ICCV 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.6% to 19.8%, and submissions increased from 966 to 1,230.
