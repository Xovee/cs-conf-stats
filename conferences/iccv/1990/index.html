ICCV 1990 Acceptance Rate

ICCV 1990 accepted 102 papers out of 420 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.3%.

International Conference on Computer Vision 1990 (3rd) was held in Osaka, Japan.

Compared with ICCV 1988, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.1% to 24.3%, and submissions increased from 306 to 420.
