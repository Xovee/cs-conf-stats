AAAI 2011 Acceptance Rate

AAAI 2011 accepted 242 papers out of 990 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.4%.

AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence 2011 (25th) was held in San Francisco, California, USA.

Compared with AAAI 2010, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.0% to 24.4%, and submissions increased from 977 to 990.
