AAAI 2021 Acceptance Rate

AAAI 2021 accepted 1,692 papers out of 7,911 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.4%.

AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence 2021 (35th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with AAAI 2020, the acceptance rate increased from 20.6% to 21.4%, and submissions increased from 7,737 to 7,911.
