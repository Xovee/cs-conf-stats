AAAI 2019 Acceptance Rate

AAAI 2019 accepted 1,150 papers out of 7,095 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.2%.

AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence 2019 (33rd) was held in Honolulu, Hawaii, USA.

Compared with AAAI 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.6% to 16.2%, and submissions increased from 3,808 to 7,095.
