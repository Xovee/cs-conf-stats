AAAI 2013 Acceptance Rate

AAAI 2013 accepted 203 papers out of 690 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.4%.

AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence 2013 (27th) was held in Bellevue, Washington, USA.

Compared with AAAI 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 26.0% to 29.4%, and submissions decreased from 1,129 to 690.
