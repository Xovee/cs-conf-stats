AAAI 2012 Acceptance Rate

AAAI 2012 accepted 294 papers out of 1,129 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.0%.

AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence 2012 (26th) was held in Toronto, Canada.

Compared with AAAI 2011, the acceptance rate increased from 24.4% to 26.0%, and submissions increased from 990 to 1,129.
