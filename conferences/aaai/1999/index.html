AAAI 1999 Acceptance Rate

AAAI 1999 accepted 109 papers out of 400 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.3%.

AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence 1999 (16th) was held in Orlando, USA.

Compared with AAAI 1998, the acceptance rate decreased from 30.1% to 27.3%, and submissions decreased from 475 to 400.
