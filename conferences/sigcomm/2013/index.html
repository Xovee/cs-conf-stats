SIGCOMM 2013 Acceptance Rate

SIGCOMM 2013 accepted 38 papers out of 246 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.4%.

Annual Conference of the ACM SIGCOMM on the Applications, Technologies, Architectures, and Protocols for Computer Communication 2013 (46th) was held in Hong Kong, China.

Compared with SIGCOMM 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 13.2% to 15.4%, and submissions increased from 235 to 246.
