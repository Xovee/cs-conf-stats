SIGCOMM 2020 Acceptance Rate

SIGCOMM 2020 accepted 53 papers out of 250 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.2%.

Annual Conference of the ACM SIGCOMM on the Applications, Technologies, Architectures, and Protocols for Computer Communication 2020 (53rd) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with SIGCOMM 2019, the acceptance rate increased from 14.3% to 21.2%, and submissions increased from 223 to 250.
