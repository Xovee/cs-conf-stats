SIGCOMM 2006 Acceptance Rate

SIGCOMM 2006 accepted 37 papers out of 298 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 12.4%.

Annual Conference of the ACM SIGCOMM on the Applications, Technologies, Architectures, and Protocols for Computer Communication 2006 (39th) was held in Pisa, Italy.

Compared with SIGCOMM 2005, the acceptance rate increased from 10.6% to 12.4%, and submissions increased from 255 to 298.
