SIGCOMM 2005 Acceptance Rate

SIGCOMM 2005 accepted 27 papers out of 255 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 10.6%.

Annual Conference of the ACM SIGCOMM on the Applications, Technologies, Architectures, and Protocols for Computer Communication 2005 (38th) was held in Philadelphia, USA.

Compared with SIGCOMM 2004, the acceptance rate increased from 9.8% to 10.6%, and submissions decreased from 297 to 255.
