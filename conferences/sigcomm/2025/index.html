SIGCOMM 2025 Acceptance Rate

SIGCOMM 2025 accepted 74 papers out of 460 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.1%.

Annual Conference of the ACM SIGCOMM on the Applications, Technologies, Architectures, and Protocols for Computer Communication 2025 (58th) was held in Coimbra, Portugal.

Compared with SIGCOMM 2024, the acceptance rate decreased from 16.9% to 16.1%, and submissions increased from 366 to 460.
