SIGCOMM 2004 Acceptance Rate

SIGCOMM 2004 accepted 29 papers out of 297 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 9.8%.

Annual Conference of the ACM SIGCOMM on the Applications, Technologies, Architectures, and Protocols for Computer Communication 2004 (37th) was held in Portland, USA.

Compared with SIGCOMM 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 10.7% to 9.8%, and submissions decreased from 319 to 297.
