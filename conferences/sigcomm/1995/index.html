SIGCOMM 1995 Acceptance Rate

SIGCOMM 1995 accepted 30 papers out of 143 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.0%.

Compared with SIGCOMM 1994, the acceptance rate increased from 20.6% to 21.0%, and submissions increased from 141 to 143.
