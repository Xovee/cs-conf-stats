SIGCOMM 2002 Acceptance Rate

SIGCOMM 2002 accepted 25 papers out of 300 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 8.3%.

Annual Conference of the ACM SIGCOMM on the Applications, Technologies, Architectures, and Protocols for Computer Communication 2002 (35th) was held in Pittsburgh, USA.

Compared with SIGCOMM 2001, the acceptance rate decreased from 9.1% to 8.3%, and submissions increased from 252 to 300.
