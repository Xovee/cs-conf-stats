SIGCOMM 1998 Acceptance Rate

SIGCOMM 1998 accepted 27 papers out of 247 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 10.9%.

Annual Conference of the ACM SIGCOMM on the Applications, Technologies, Architectures, and Protocols for Computer Communication 1998 (31st) was held in Vancouver, Canada.

Compared with SIGCOMM 1997, the acceptance rate decreased from 11.3% to 10.9%, and submissions increased from 213 to 247.
