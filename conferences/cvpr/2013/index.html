CVPR 2013 Acceptance Rate

CVPR 2013 accepted 472 papers out of 1,798 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.3%.

International Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition 2013 (26th) was held in Portland, USA.

Compared with CVPR 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 26.2% to 26.3%, and submissions increased from 1,776 to 1,798.
