CVPR 2019 Acceptance Rate

CVPR 2019 accepted 1,300 papers out of 5,165 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.2%.

International Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition 2019 (32nd) was held in Long Beach, California, USA.

Compared with CVPR 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.6% to 25.2%, and submissions increased from 3,303 to 5,165.
