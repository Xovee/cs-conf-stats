CVPR 2014 Acceptance Rate

CVPR 2014 accepted 540 papers out of 1,778 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.4%.

International Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition 2014 (27th) was held in Columbus, USA.

Compared with CVPR 2013, the acceptance rate increased from 26.3% to 30.4%, and submissions decreased from 1,798 to 1,778.
