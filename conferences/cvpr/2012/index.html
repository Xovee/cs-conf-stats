CVPR 2012 Acceptance Rate

CVPR 2012 accepted 465 papers out of 1,776 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.2%.

International Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition 2012 (25th) was held in Providence, Rhode Island, USA.

Compared with CVPR 2011, the acceptance rate increased from 25.9% to 26.2%, and submissions increased from 1,688 to 1,776.
