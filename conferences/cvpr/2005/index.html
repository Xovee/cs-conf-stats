CVPR 2005 Acceptance Rate

CVPR 2005 accepted 324 papers out of 1,160 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.9%.

International Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition 2005 (18th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with CVPR 2004, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.8% to 27.9%, and submissions increased from 873 to 1,160.
