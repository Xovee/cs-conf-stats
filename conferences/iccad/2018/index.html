ICCAD 2018 Acceptance Rate

ICCAD 2018 accepted 98 papers out of 400 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.5%.

IEEE/ACM International Conference on Computer Aided Design 2018 (36th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with ICCAD 2017, the acceptance rate decreased from 26.3% to 24.5%, and submissions increased from 399 to 400.
