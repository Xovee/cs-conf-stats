ICCAD 2014 Acceptance Rate

ICCAD 2014 accepted 77 papers out of 304 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.3%.

IEEE/ACM International Conference on Computer Aided Design 2014 (32nd) was held in San Jose, California, USA.

Compared with ICCAD 2013, the acceptance rate decreased from 26.0% to 25.3%, and submissions decreased from 354 to 304.
