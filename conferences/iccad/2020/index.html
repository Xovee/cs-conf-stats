ICCAD 2020 Acceptance Rate

ICCAD 2020 accepted 127 papers out of 470 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.0%.

IEEE/ACM International Conference on Computer Aided Design 2020 (38th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with ICCAD 2019, the acceptance rate increased from 23.9% to 27.0%, and submissions increased from 394 to 470.
