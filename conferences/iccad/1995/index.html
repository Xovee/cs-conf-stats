ICCAD 1995 Acceptance Rate

ICCAD 1995 accepted 108 papers out of 350 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.9%.

IEEE/ACM International Conference on Computer Aided Design 1995 (13th) was held in San Jose, California, USA.

Compared with ICCAD 1993, the acceptance rate increased from 30.7% to 30.9%, and submissions decreased from 420 to 350.
