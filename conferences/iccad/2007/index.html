ICCAD 2007 Acceptance Rate

ICCAD 2007 accepted 139 papers out of 510 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.3%.

IEEE/ACM International Conference on Computer Aided Design 2007 (25th) was held in San Jose, California, USA.

Compared with ICCAD 2005, the acceptance rate increased from 23.7% to 27.3%, and submissions decreased from 540 to 510.
