SODA 2014 Acceptance Rate

SODA 2014 accepted 136 papers out of 485 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.0%.

ACM-SIAM Symposium on Discrete Algorithms 2014 (25th) was held in Portland, USA.

Compared with SODA 2013, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.7% to 28.0%, and submissions increased from 454 to 485.
