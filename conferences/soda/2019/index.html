SODA 2019 Acceptance Rate

SODA 2019 accepted 184 papers out of 586 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 31.4%.

ACM-SIAM Symposium on Discrete Algorithms 2019 (30th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with SODA 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 33.1% to 31.4%, and submissions increased from 547 to 586.
