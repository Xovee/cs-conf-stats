SODA 2003 Acceptance Rate

SODA 2003 accepted 92 papers out of 331 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.8%.

Compared with SODA 2002, the acceptance rate decreased from 38.9% to 27.8%, and submissions increased from 283 to 331.
