SODA 2004 Acceptance Rate

SODA 2004 accepted 117 papers out of 360 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 32.5%.

ACM-SIAM Symposium on Discrete Algorithms 2004 (15th) was held in New Orleans, USA.

Compared with SODA 2003, the acceptance rate increased from 27.8% to 32.5%, and submissions increased from 331 to 360.
