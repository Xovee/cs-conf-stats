SODA 2020 Acceptance Rate

SODA 2020 accepted 182 papers out of 589 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.9%.

ACM-SIAM Symposium on Discrete Algorithms 2020 (31st) was held in Salt Lake City, USA.

Compared with SODA 2019, the acceptance rate decreased from 31.4% to 30.9%, and submissions increased from 586 to 589.
