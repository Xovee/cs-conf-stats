SODA 2015 Acceptance Rate

SODA 2015 accepted 137 papers out of 495 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.7%.

ACM-SIAM Symposium on Discrete Algorithms 2015 (26th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with SODA 2014, the acceptance rate decreased from 28.0% to 27.7%, and submissions increased from 485 to 495.
