SenSys 2010 Acceptance Rate

SenSys 2010 accepted 25 papers out of 145 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.2%.

ACM Conference on Embedded Networked Sensor Systems 2010 (8th) was held in Zurich, Switzerland.

Compared with SenSys 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 17.6% to 17.2%, and submissions increased from 119 to 145.
