SenSys 2022 Acceptance Rate

SenSys 2022 accepted 52 papers out of 209 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.9%.

ACM Conference on Embedded Networked Sensor Systems 2022 (20th) was held in Boston, USA.

Compared with SenSys 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 18.0% to 24.9%, and submissions increased from 139 to 209.
