SenSys 2021 Acceptance Rate

SenSys 2021 accepted 25 papers out of 139 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.0%.

ACM Conference on Embedded Networked Sensor Systems 2021 (19th) was held in Coimbra, Portugal.

Compared with SenSys 2020, the acceptance rate decreased from 21.2% to 18.0%, and submissions decreased from 203 to 139.
