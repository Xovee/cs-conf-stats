SenSys 2017 Acceptance Rate

SenSys 2017 accepted 26 papers out of 152 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.1%.

ACM Conference on Embedded Networked Sensor Systems 2017 (15th) was held in Delft, The Netherlands.

Compared with SenSys 2016, the acceptance rate decreased from 17.6% to 17.1%, and submissions increased from 119 to 152.
