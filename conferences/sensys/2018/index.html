SenSys 2018 Acceptance Rate

SenSys 2018 accepted 23 papers out of 147 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.6%.

ACM Conference on Embedded Networked Sensor Systems 2018 (16th) was held in Shenzhen, China.

Compared with SenSys 2017, the acceptance rate decreased from 17.1% to 15.6%, and submissions decreased from 152 to 147.
