ECIR 2012 Acceptance Rate

ECIR 2012 accepted 37 papers out of 163 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.7%.

European Conference on Information Retrieval 2012 (34th) was held in Barcelona, Spain.

Compared with ECIR 2011, the acceptance rate increased from 20.2% to 22.7%, and submissions decreased from 223 to 163.
