ECIR 2023 Acceptance Rate

ECIR 2023 accepted 57 papers out of 224 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.4%.

European Conference on Information Retrieval 2023 (45th) was held in Dublin, Ireland.

Compared with ECIR 2022, the acceptance rate increased from 20.0% to 25.4%, and submissions increased from 175 to 224.
