ECIR 2010 Acceptance Rate

ECIR 2010 accepted 44 papers out of 202 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.8%.

European Conference on Information Retrieval 2010 (32nd) was held in Milton Keynes, UK.

Compared with ECIR 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 22.3% to 21.8%, and submissions increased from 188 to 202.
