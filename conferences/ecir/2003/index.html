ECIR 2003 Acceptance Rate

ECIR 2003 accepted 31 papers out of 101 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.7%.

European Conference on Information Retrieval 2003 (25th) was held in Pisa, Italy.

Compared with ECIR 2002, the acceptance rate decreased from 44.2% to 30.7%, and submissions increased from 52 to 101.
