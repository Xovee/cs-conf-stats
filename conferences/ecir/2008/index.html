ECIR 2008 Acceptance Rate

ECIR 2008 accepted 33 papers out of 139 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.7%.

European Conference on Information Retrieval 2008 (30th) was held in Glasgow, Scotland, UK.

Compared with ECIR 2007, the acceptance rate increased from 19.1% to 23.7%, and submissions decreased from 220 to 139.
