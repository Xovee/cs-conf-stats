FOCS 2022 Acceptance Rate

FOCS 2022 accepted 110 papers out of 296 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 37.2%.

IEEE Annual Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science 2022 (63rd) was held in Denver, USA.

Compared with FOCS 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 34.4% to 37.2%, and submissions decreased from 340 to 296.
