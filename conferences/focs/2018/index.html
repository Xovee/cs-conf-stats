FOCS 2018 Acceptance Rate

FOCS 2018 accepted 86 papers out of 320 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.9%.

IEEE Annual Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science 2018 (59th) was held in Paris, France.

Compared with FOCS 2017, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.9% to 26.9%, and submissions decreased from 323 to 320.
