FOCS 2010 Acceptance Rate

FOCS 2010 accepted 81 papers out of 270 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.0%.

IEEE Annual Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science 2010 (51st) was held in Las Vegas, USA.

Compared with FOCS 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 30.1% to 30.0%, and submissions increased from 249 to 270.
