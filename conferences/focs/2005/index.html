FOCS 2005 Acceptance Rate

FOCS 2005 accepted 62 papers out of 276 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.5%.

IEEE Annual Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science 2005 (46th) was held in Pittsburgh, USA.

Compared with FOCS 2004, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.5% to 22.5%, and submissions increased from 272 to 276.
