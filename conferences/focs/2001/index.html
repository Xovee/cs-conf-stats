FOCS 2001 Acceptance Rate

FOCS 2001 accepted 63 papers out of 214 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.4%.

IEEE Annual Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science 2001 (42nd) was held in Las Vegas, USA.

Compared with FOCS 2000, the acceptance rate decreased from 32.0% to 29.4%, and submissions increased from 206 to 214.
