FOCS 2008 Acceptance Rate

FOCS 2008 accepted 79 papers out of 276 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.6%.

IEEE Annual Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science 2008 (49th) was held in Philadelphia, USA.

Compared with FOCS 2007, the acceptance rate increased from 21.9% to 28.6%, and submissions decreased from 302 to 276.
