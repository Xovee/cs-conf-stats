FOCS 1998 Acceptance Rate

FOCS 1998 accepted 76 papers out of 204 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 37.3%.

IEEE Annual Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science 1998 (39th) was held in Palo Alto, California, USA.

Compared with FOCS 1996, the acceptance rate increased from 36.2% to 37.3%, and submissions increased from 174 to 204.
