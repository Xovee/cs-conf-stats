FOCS 2024 Acceptance Rate

FOCS 2024 accepted 131 papers out of 500 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.2%.

IEEE Annual Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science 2024 (65th) was held in Chicago, USA.

Compared with FOCS 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 33.5% to 26.2%, and submissions increased from 421 to 500.
