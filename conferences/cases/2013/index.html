CASES 2013 Acceptance Rate

CASES 2013 accepted 21 papers out of 68 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.9%.

International Conference on Compilers, Architectures, and Synthesis for Embedded Systems 2013 (16th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with CASES 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 30.0% to 30.9%, and submissions increased from 60 to 68.
