CASES 2002 Acceptance Rate

CASES 2002 accepted 38 papers out of 118 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 32.2%.

International Conference on Compilers, Architectures, and Synthesis for Embedded Systems 2002 (5th) was held in Grenoble, France.

Compared with CASES 2001, the acceptance rate decreased from 35.0% to 32.2%, and submissions increased from 80 to 118.
