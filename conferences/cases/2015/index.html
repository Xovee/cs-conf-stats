CASES 2015 Acceptance Rate

CASES 2015 accepted 18 papers out of 49 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 36.7%.

International Conference on Compilers, Architectures, and Synthesis for Embedded Systems 2015 (18th) was held in Amsterdam, The Netherlands.

Compared with CASES 2014, the acceptance rate increased from 30.0% to 36.7%, and submissions decreased from 80 to 49.
