ICRA 2024 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 2024 accepted 1,760 papers out of 3,937 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 44.7%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2024 (41st) was held in Yokohama, Japan.

Compared with ICRA 2023, the acceptance rate increased from 43.0% to 44.7%, and submissions increased from 3,125 to 3,937.
