ICRA 1987 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 1987 accepted 350 papers out of 583 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 60.0%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 1987 (4th) was held in Raleigh, USA.

Compared with ICRA 1986, the acceptance rate decreased from 70.0% to 60.0%, and submissions increased from 486 to 583.
