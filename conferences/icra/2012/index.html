ICRA 2012 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 2012 accepted 820 papers out of 2,020 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 40.6%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2012 (29th) was held in Saint Paul, Minnesota, USA.

Compared with ICRA 2011, the acceptance rate decreased from 51.8% to 40.6%, and submissions increased from 1,980 to 2,020.
