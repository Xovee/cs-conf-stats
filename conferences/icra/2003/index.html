ICRA 2003 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 2003 accepted 714 papers out of 1,176 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 60.7%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2003 (20th) was held in Taipei, Taiwan, China.

Compared with ICRA 2002, the acceptance rate increased from 58.8% to 60.7%, and submissions increased from 1,172 to 1,176.
