ICRA 2010 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 2010 accepted 853 papers out of 2,020 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 42.2%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2010 (27th) was held in Anchorage, USA.

Compared with ICRA 2008, the acceptance rate decreased from 44.7% to 42.2%, and submissions increased from 1,478 to 2,020.
