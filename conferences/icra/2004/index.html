ICRA 2004 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 2004 accepted 858 papers out of 1,457 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 58.9%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2004 (21st) was held in New Orleans, USA.

Compared with ICRA 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 60.7% to 58.9%, and submissions increased from 1,176 to 1,457.
