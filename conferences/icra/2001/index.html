ICRA 2001 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 2001 accepted 678 papers out of 1,150 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 59.0%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2001 (18th) was held in Seoul, Korea.

Compared with ICRA 2000, the acceptance rate increased from 58.3% to 59.0%, and submissions increased from 1,100 to 1,150.
