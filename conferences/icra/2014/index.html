ICRA 2014 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 2014 accepted 1,015 papers out of 2,090 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 48.6%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2014 (31st) was held in Hong Kong, China.

Compared with ICRA 2013, the acceptance rate increased from 38.3% to 48.6%, and submissions decreased from 2,270 to 2,090.
