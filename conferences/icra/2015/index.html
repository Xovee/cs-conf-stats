ICRA 2015 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 2015 accepted 940 papers out of 2,280 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 41.2%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2015 (32nd) was held in Seattle, USA.

Compared with ICRA 2014, the acceptance rate decreased from 48.6% to 41.2%, and submissions increased from 2,090 to 2,280.
