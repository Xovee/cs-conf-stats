ICRA 1998 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 1998 accepted 596 papers out of 963 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 61.9%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 1998 (15th) was held in Leuven, Belgium.

Compared with ICRA 1997, the acceptance rate decreased from 63.8% to 61.9%, and submissions increased from 900 to 963.
