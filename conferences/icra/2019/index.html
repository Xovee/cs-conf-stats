ICRA 2019 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 2019 accepted 1,317 papers out of 2,916 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 45.2%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2019 (36th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with ICRA 2018, the acceptance rate increased from 40.8% to 45.2%, and submissions increased from 2,586 to 2,916.
