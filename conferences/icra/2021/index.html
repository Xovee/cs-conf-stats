ICRA 2021 Acceptance Rate

ICRA 2021 accepted 1,690 papers out of 3,877 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 43.6%.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation 2021 (38th) was held in Xi'an, China.

Compared with ICRA 2020, the acceptance rate decreased from 44.0% to 43.6%, and submissions increased from 2,902 to 3,877.
