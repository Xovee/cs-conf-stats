ECRTS 2004 Acceptance Rate

ECRTS 2004 accepted 29 papers out of 115 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.2%.

Euromicro Conference on Real-Time Systems 2004 (16th) was held in Catania, Italy.

Compared with ECRTS 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 31.1% to 25.2%, and submissions increased from 103 to 115.
