ECRTS 2016 Acceptance Rate

ECRTS 2016 accepted 24 papers out of 92 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.1%.

Euromicro Conference on Real-Time Systems 2016 (28th) was held in Toulouse, France.

Compared with ECRTS 2015, the acceptance rate decreased from 31.3% to 26.1%, and submissions increased from 80 to 92.
