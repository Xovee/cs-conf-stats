ECRTS 1996 Acceptance Rate

ECRTS 1996 accepted 48 papers out of 77 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 62.3%.

Compared with ECRTS 1992, the acceptance rate increased from 53.3% to 62.3%, and submissions increased from 60 to 77.
