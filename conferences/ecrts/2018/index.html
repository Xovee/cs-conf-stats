ECRTS 2018 Acceptance Rate

ECRTS 2018 accepted 26 papers out of 78 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 33.3%.

Euromicro Conference on Real-Time Systems 2018 (30th) was held in Barcelona, Spain.

Compared with ECRTS 2017, the acceptance rate increased from 32.9% to 33.3%, and submissions decreased from 79 to 78.
