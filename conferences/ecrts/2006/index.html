ECRTS 2006 Acceptance Rate

ECRTS 2006 accepted 25 papers out of 105 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.8%.

Euromicro Conference on Real-Time Systems 2006 (18th) was held in Dresden, Germany.

Compared with ECRTS 2005, the acceptance rate increased from 17.9% to 23.8%, and submissions decreased from 145 to 105.
