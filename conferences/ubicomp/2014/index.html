UbiComp 2014 Acceptance Rate

UbiComp 2014 accepted 71 papers out of 454 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.6%.

ACM International Joint Conference on Pervasive and Ubiquitous Computing 2014 (14th) was held in Seattle, USA.

Compared with UbiComp 2013, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.4% to 15.6%, and submissions increased from 394 to 454.
