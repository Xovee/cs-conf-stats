UbiComp 2007 Acceptance Rate

UbiComp 2007 accepted 29 papers out of 152 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.1%.

ACM International Joint Conference on Pervasive and Ubiquitous Computing 2007 (7th) was held in Innsbruck, Austria.

Compared with UbiComp 2006, the acceptance rate increased from 14.7% to 19.1%, and submissions decreased from 204 to 152.
