NDSS 2019 Acceptance Rate

NDSS 2019 accepted 89 papers out of 521 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.1%.

Network and Distributed System Security Symposium 2019 (26th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with NDSS 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 21.5% to 17.1%, and submissions increased from 331 to 521.
