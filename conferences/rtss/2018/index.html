RTSS 2018 Acceptance Rate

RTSS 2018 accepted 37 papers out of 166 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.3%.

IEEE Real-Time Systems Symposium 2018 (39th) was held in Nashville, USA.

Compared with RTSS 2017, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.7% to 22.3%, and submissions increased from 131 to 166.
