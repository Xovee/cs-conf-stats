RTSS 2006 Acceptance Rate

RTSS 2006 accepted 42 papers out of 173 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.3%.

IEEE Real-Time Systems Symposium 2006 (27th) was held in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Compared with RTSS 2005, the acceptance rate increased from 21.0% to 24.3%, and submissions decreased from 176 to 173.
