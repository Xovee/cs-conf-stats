RTSS 2013 Acceptance Rate

RTSS 2013 accepted 36 papers out of 160 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.5%.

IEEE Real-Time Systems Symposium 2013 (34th) was held in Vancouver, Canada.

Compared with RTSS 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 22.3% to 22.5%, and submissions increased from 157 to 160.
