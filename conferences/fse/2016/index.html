FSE 2016 Acceptance Rate

FSE 2016 accepted 74 papers out of 266 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.8%.

ACM International Conference on the Foundations of Software Engineering 2016 (24th) was held in Seattle, USA.

Compared with FSE 2015, the acceptance rate increased from 25.4% to 27.8%, and submissions decreased from 291 to 266.
