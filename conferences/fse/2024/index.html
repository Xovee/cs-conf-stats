FSE 2024 Acceptance Rate

FSE 2024 accepted 121 papers out of 474 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.5%.

ACM International Conference on the Foundations of Software Engineering 2024 (32nd) was held in Porto de Galinhas, Brazil.

Compared with FSE 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.3% to 25.5%, and submissions increased from 465 to 474.
