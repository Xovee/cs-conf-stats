FSE 2014 Acceptance Rate

FSE 2014 accepted 61 papers out of 280 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.8%.

ACM International Conference on the Foundations of Software Engineering 2014 (22nd) was held in Hong Kong, China.

Compared with FSE 2013, the acceptance rate increased from 20.3% to 21.8%, and submissions increased from 251 to 280.
