FSE 2015 Acceptance Rate

FSE 2015 accepted 74 papers out of 291 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.4%.

ACM International Conference on the Foundations of Software Engineering 2015 (23rd) was held in Bergamo, Italy.

Compared with FSE 2014, the acceptance rate increased from 21.8% to 25.4%, and submissions increased from 280 to 291.
