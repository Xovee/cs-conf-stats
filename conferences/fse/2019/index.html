FSE 2019 Acceptance Rate

FSE 2019 accepted 74 papers out of 303 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.4%.

ACM International Conference on the Foundations of Software Engineering 2019 (27th) was held in Tallinn, Estonia.

Compared with FSE 2018, the acceptance rate increased from 21.1% to 24.4%, and submissions increased from 289 to 303.
