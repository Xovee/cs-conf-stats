FSE 2003 Acceptance Rate

FSE 2003 accepted 33 papers out of 168 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.6%.

ACM International Conference on the Foundations of Software Engineering 2003 (11th) was held in Helsinki, Finland.

Compared with FSE 2002, the acceptance rate increased from 13.3% to 19.6%, and submissions increased from 128 to 168.
