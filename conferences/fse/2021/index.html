FSE 2021 Acceptance Rate

FSE 2021 accepted 97 papers out of 396 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.5%.

ACM International Conference on the Foundations of Software Engineering 2021 (29th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with FSE 2020, the acceptance rate decreased from 28.1% to 24.5%, and submissions increased from 360 to 396.
