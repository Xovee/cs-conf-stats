DAC 2011 Acceptance Rate

DAC 2011 accepted 156 papers out of 690 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.6%.

Design Automation Conference 2011 (48th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with DAC 2010, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.4% to 22.6%, and submissions increased from 607 to 690.
