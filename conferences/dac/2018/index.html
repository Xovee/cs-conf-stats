DAC 2018 Acceptance Rate

DAC 2018 accepted 168 papers out of 691 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.3%.

Design Automation Conference 2018 (55th) was held in San Francisco, California, USA.

Compared with DAC 2013, the acceptance rate increased from 20.9% to 24.3%, and submissions decreased from 740 to 691.
