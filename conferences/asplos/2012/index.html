ASPLOS 2012 Acceptance Rate

ASPLOS 2012 accepted 37 papers out of 172 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.5%.

ACM International Conference on Architectural Support for Programming Languages and Operating Systems 2012 (17th) was held in London, UK.

Compared with ASPLOS 2011, the acceptance rate increased from 21.1% to 21.5%, and submissions increased from 152 to 172.
