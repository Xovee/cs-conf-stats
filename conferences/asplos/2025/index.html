ASPLOS 2025 Acceptance Rate

ASPLOS 2025 accepted 177 papers out of 912 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.4%.

ACM International Conference on Architectural Support for Programming Languages and Operating Systems 2025 (29th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with ASPLOS 2024, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.9% to 19.4%, and submissions decreased from 922 to 912.
