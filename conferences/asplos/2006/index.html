ASPLOS 2006 Acceptance Rate

ASPLOS 2006 accepted 38 papers out of 158 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.1%.

ACM International Conference on Architectural Support for Programming Languages and Operating Systems 2006 (12th) was held in San Jose, California, USA.

Compared with ASPLOS 2004, the acceptance rate increased from 14.2% to 24.1%, and submissions decreased from 169 to 158.
