ASPLOS 1994 Acceptance Rate

ASPLOS 1994 accepted 29 papers out of 146 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.9%.

ACM International Conference on Architectural Support for Programming Languages and Operating Systems 1994 (6th) was held in San Jose, California, USA.

Compared with ASPLOS 1992, the acceptance rate increased from 16.0% to 19.9%, and submissions decreased from 150 to 146.
