ISSTA 2013 Acceptance Rate

ISSTA 2013 accepted 32 papers out of 124 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.8%.

ACM SIGSOFT International Symposium on Software Testing and Analysis 2013 (22nd) was held in Lugano, Switzerland.

Compared with ISSTA 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 28.7% to 25.8%, and submissions increased from 108 to 124.
