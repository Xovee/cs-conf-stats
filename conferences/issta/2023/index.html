ISSTA 2023 Acceptance Rate

ISSTA 2023 accepted 117 papers out of 406 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.8%.

ACM SIGSOFT International Symposium on Software Testing and Analysis 2023 (32nd) was held in Seattle, USA.

Compared with ISSTA 2022, the acceptance rate increased from 24.4% to 28.8%, and submissions increased from 250 to 406.
