ISSTA 2016 Acceptance Rate

ISSTA 2016 accepted 37 papers out of 147 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.2%.

ACM SIGSOFT International Symposium on Software Testing and Analysis 2016 (25th) was held in Saarbrucken, Germany.

Compared with ISSTA 2015, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.7% to 25.2%, and submissions increased from 119 to 147.
