ISSTA 2018 Acceptance Rate

ISSTA 2018 accepted 31 papers out of 132 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.5%.

ACM SIGSOFT International Symposium on Software Testing and Analysis 2018 (27th) was held in Amsterdam, The Netherlands.

Compared with ISSTA 2017, the acceptance rate decreased from 26.3% to 23.5%, and submissions increased from 118 to 132.
