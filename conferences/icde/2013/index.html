ICDE 2013 Acceptance Rate

ICDE 2013 accepted 95 papers out of 443 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.4%.

IEEE International Conference on Data Engineering 2013 (29th) was held in Brisbane, Australia.

Compared with ICDE 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.2% to 21.4%, and submissions increased from 413 to 443.
