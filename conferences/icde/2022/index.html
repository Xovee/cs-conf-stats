ICDE 2022 Acceptance Rate

ICDE 2022 accepted 211 papers out of 780 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.1%.

IEEE International Conference on Data Engineering 2022 (38th) was held in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

Compared with ICDE 2021, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.5% to 27.1%, and submissions increased from 549 to 780.
