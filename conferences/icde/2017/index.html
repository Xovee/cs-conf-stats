ICDE 2017 Acceptance Rate

ICDE 2017 accepted 63 papers out of 356 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.7%.

IEEE International Conference on Data Engineering 2017 (33rd) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with ICDE 2016, the acceptance rate decreased from 25.1% to 17.7%, and submissions decreased from 395 to 356.
