ICDE 2019 Acceptance Rate

ICDE 2019 accepted 129 papers out of 568 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.7%.

IEEE International Conference on Data Engineering 2019 (35th) was held in Macau, China.

Compared with ICDE 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.1% to 22.7%, and submissions increased from 424 to 568.
