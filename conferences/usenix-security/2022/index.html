USENIX Security 2022 Acceptance Rate

USENIX Security 2022 accepted 256 papers out of 1,414 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.1%.

USENIX Security Symposium. 2022 (31st) was held in Boston, USA.

Compared with USENIX Security 2021, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.0% to 18.1%, and submissions increased from 1,294 to 1,414.
