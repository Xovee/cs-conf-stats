USENIX Security 2005 Acceptance Rate

USENIX Security 2005 accepted 22 papers out of 149 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 14.8%.

USENIX Security Symposium. 2005 (14th) was held in Baltimore, USA.

Compared with USENIX Security 2004, the acceptance rate increased from 12.0% to 14.8%, and submissions decreased from 184 to 149.
