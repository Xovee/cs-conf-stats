USENIX Security 2009 Acceptance Rate

USENIX Security 2009 accepted 26 papers out of 172 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.1%.

USENIX Security Symposium. 2009 (18th) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with USENIX Security 2008, the acceptance rate decreased from 15.9% to 15.1%, and submissions increased from 170 to 172.
