USENIX Security 2025 Acceptance Rate

USENIX Security 2025 accepted 407 papers out of 2,385 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.1%.

USENIX Security Symposium. 2025 (34th) was held in Seattle, USA.

Compared with USENIX Security 2024, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.2% to 17.1%, and submissions increased from 2,176 to 2,385.
