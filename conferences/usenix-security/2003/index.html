USENIX Security 2003 Acceptance Rate

USENIX Security 2003 accepted 21 papers out of 128 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.4%.

USENIX Security Symposium. 2003 (12th) was held in Washington DC, USA.

Compared with USENIX Security 2002, the acceptance rate decreased from 16.9% to 16.4%, and submissions decreased from 130 to 128.
