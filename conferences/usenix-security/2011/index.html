USENIX Security 2011 Acceptance Rate

USENIX Security 2011 accepted 35 papers out of 204 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.2%.

USENIX Security Symposium. 2011 (20th) was held in San Francisco, California, USA.

Compared with USENIX Security 2010, the acceptance rate increased from 14.9% to 17.2%, and submissions increased from 202 to 204.
