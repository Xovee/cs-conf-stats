MobiCom 2019 Acceptance Rate

MobiCom 2019 accepted 55 papers out of 290 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.0%.

International Conference on Mobile Computing and Networking 2019 (25th) was held in Los Cabos, Mexico.

Compared with MobiCom 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 22.5% to 19.0%, and submissions increased from 187 to 290.
