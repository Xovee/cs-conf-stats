ACM MM 2020 Acceptance Rate

ACM MM 2020 accepted 474 papers out of 1,698 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.9%.

ACM International Conference on Multimedia 2020 (28th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with ACM MM 2019, the acceptance rate increased from 26.9% to 27.9%, and submissions increased from 936 to 1,698.
