ACM MM 2024 Acceptance Rate

ACM MM 2024 accepted 1,150 papers out of 4,385 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.2%.

ACM International Conference on Multimedia 2024 (32nd) was held in Melbourne, Australia.

Compared with ACM MM 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 29.4% to 26.2%, and submissions increased from 3,072 to 4,385.
