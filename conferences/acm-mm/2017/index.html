ACM MM 2017 Acceptance Rate

ACM MM 2017 accepted 189 papers out of 684 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.6%.

ACM International Conference on Multimedia 2017 (25th) was held in Mountain View, California, USA.

Compared with ACM MM 2016, the acceptance rate increased from 21.9% to 27.6%, and submissions increased from 237 to 684.
