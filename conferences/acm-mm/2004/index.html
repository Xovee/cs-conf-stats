ACM MM 2004 Acceptance Rate

ACM MM 2004 accepted 55 papers out of 331 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.6%.

ACM International Conference on Multimedia 2004 (12th) was held in New York City, USA.

Compared with ACM MM 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 16.9% to 16.6%, and submissions increased from 255 to 331.
