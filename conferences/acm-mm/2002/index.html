ACM MM 2002 Acceptance Rate

ACM MM 2002 accepted 46 papers out of 330 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 13.9%.

ACM International Conference on Multimedia 2002 (10th) was held in Juan-les-Pins, France.

Compared with ACM MM 1997, the acceptance rate decreased from 28.2% to 13.9%, and submissions increased from 142 to 330.
