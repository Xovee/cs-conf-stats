CSCW 2018 Acceptance Rate

CSCW 2018 accepted 289 papers out of 1,107 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.1%.

ACM Conference on Computer-Supported Cooperative Work and Social Computing 2018 (21st) was held in Jersey City, USA.

Compared with CSCW 2017, the acceptance rate decreased from 34.5% to 26.1%, and submissions increased from 530 to 1,107.
