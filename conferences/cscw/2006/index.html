CSCW 2006 Acceptance Rate

CSCW 2006 accepted 62 papers out of 304 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 20.4%.

ACM Conference on Computer-Supported Cooperative Work and Social Computing 2006 (11th) was held in Banff, Canada.

Compared with CSCW 2004, the acceptance rate decreased from 30.1% to 20.4%, and submissions increased from 176 to 304.
