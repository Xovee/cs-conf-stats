CSCW 1998 Acceptance Rate

CSCW 1998 accepted 41 papers out of 220 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.6%.

ACM Conference on Computer-Supported Cooperative Work and Social Computing 1998 (7th) was held in Seattle, USA.

Compared with CSCW 1994, the acceptance rate decreased from 21.0% to 18.6%, and submissions increased from 200 to 220.
