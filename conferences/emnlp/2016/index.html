EMNLP 2016 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2016 accepted 264 papers out of 1,087 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.3%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2016 (21st) was held in Austin, USA.

Compared with EMNLP 2015, the acceptance rate increased from 24.0% to 24.3%, and submissions decreased from 1,300 to 1,087.
