EMNLP 2010 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2010 accepted 125 papers out of 500 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.0%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2010 (15th) was held in Cambridge (MA), USA.

Compared with EMNLP 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 34.3% to 25.0%, and submissions increased from 475 to 500.
