EMNLP 2009 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2009 accepted 163 papers out of 475 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 34.3%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2009 (14th) was held in Singapore, Singapore.

Compared with EMNLP 2008, the acceptance rate increased from 30.1% to 34.3%, and submissions increased from 385 to 475.
