EMNLP 2013 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2013 accepted 205 papers out of 772 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.6%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2013 (18th) was held in Seattle, USA.

Compared with EMNLP 2012, the acceptance rate increased from 24.4% to 26.6%, and submissions increased from 570 to 772.
