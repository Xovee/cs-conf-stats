EMNLP 2018 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2018 accepted 549 papers out of 2,231 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.6%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2018 (23rd) was held in Brussels, Belgium.

Compared with EMNLP 2017, the acceptance rate increased from 22.8% to 24.6%, and submissions increased from 1,418 to 2,231.
