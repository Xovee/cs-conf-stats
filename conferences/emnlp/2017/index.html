EMNLP 2017 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2017 accepted 323 papers out of 1,418 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.8%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2017 (22nd) was held in Copenhagen, Denmark.

Compared with EMNLP 2016, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.3% to 22.8%, and submissions increased from 1,087 to 1,418.
