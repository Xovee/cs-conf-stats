EMNLP 2020 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2020 accepted 752 papers out of 3,359 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.4%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2020 (25th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with EMNLP 2019, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.7% to 22.4%, and submissions increased from 2,876 to 3,359.
