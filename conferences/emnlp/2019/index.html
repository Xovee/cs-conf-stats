EMNLP 2019 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2019 accepted 683 papers out of 2,876 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.7%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2019 (24th) was held in Hong Kong, China.

Compared with EMNLP 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.6% to 23.7%, and submissions increased from 2,231 to 2,876.
