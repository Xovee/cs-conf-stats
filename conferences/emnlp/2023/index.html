EMNLP 2023 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2023 accepted 1,047 papers out of 4,909 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.3%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2023 (28th) was held in Singapore, Singapore.

Compared with EMNLP 2022, the acceptance rate increased from 19.8% to 21.3%, and submissions increased from 4,190 to 4,909.
