EMNLP 2007 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2007 accepted 109 papers out of 398 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.4%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2007 (12th) was held in Prague, Czech Republic.

Compared with EMNLP 2006, the acceptance rate decreased from 31.2% to 27.4%, and submissions increased from 234 to 398.
