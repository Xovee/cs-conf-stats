EMNLP 2006 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2006 accepted 73 papers out of 234 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 31.2%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2006 (11th) was held in Sydney, Australia.

Compared with EMNLP 2005, the acceptance rate decreased from 31.6% to 31.2%, and submissions decreased from 402 to 234.
