EMNLP 2014 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2014 accepted 225 papers out of 790 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.5%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2014 (19th) was held in Doha, Qatar.

Compared with EMNLP 2013, the acceptance rate increased from 26.6% to 28.5%, and submissions increased from 772 to 790.
