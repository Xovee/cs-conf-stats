EMNLP 2015 Acceptance Rate

EMNLP 2015 accepted 312 papers out of 1,300 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.0%.

Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing 2015 (20th) was held in Lisbon, Portugal.

Compared with EMNLP 2014, the acceptance rate decreased from 28.5% to 24.0%, and submissions increased from 790 to 1,300.
