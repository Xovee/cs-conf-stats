SIGMOD 1987 Acceptance Rate

SIGMOD 1987 accepted 42 papers out of 178 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.6%.

ACM SIGMOD/PODS International Conference on Management of Data 1987 (13th) was held in San Francisco, California, USA.

Compared with SIGMOD 1986, the acceptance rate increased from 19.0% to 23.6%, and submissions increased from 158 to 178.
