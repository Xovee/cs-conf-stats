SIGMOD 1996 Acceptance Rate

SIGMOD 1996 accepted 47 papers out of 290 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.2%.

ACM SIGMOD/PODS International Conference on Management of Data 1996 (22nd) was held in Montreal, Canada.

Compared with SIGMOD 1994, the acceptance rate increased from 14.3% to 16.2%, and submissions increased from 279 to 290.
