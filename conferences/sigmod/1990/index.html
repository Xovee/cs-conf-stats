SIGMOD 1990 Acceptance Rate

SIGMOD 1990 accepted 37 papers out of 206 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.0%.

ACM SIGMOD/PODS International Conference on Management of Data 1990 (16th) was held in Atlantic City, USA.

Compared with SIGMOD 1988, the acceptance rate decreased from 22.7% to 18.0%, and submissions decreased from 211 to 206.
