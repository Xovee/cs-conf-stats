SIGMOD 2003 Acceptance Rate

SIGMOD 2003 accepted 53 papers out of 342 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.5%.

ACM SIGMOD/PODS International Conference on Management of Data 2003 (29th) was held in San Diego, California, USA.

Compared with SIGMOD 2002, the acceptance rate decreased from 17.5% to 15.5%, and submissions increased from 240 to 342.
