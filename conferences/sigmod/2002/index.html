SIGMOD 2002 Acceptance Rate

SIGMOD 2002 accepted 42 papers out of 240 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 17.5%.

ACM SIGMOD/PODS International Conference on Management of Data 2002 (28th) was held in Madison, USA.

Compared with SIGMOD 2001, the acceptance rate increased from 15.0% to 17.5%, and submissions decreased from 293 to 240.
