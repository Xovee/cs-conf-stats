SIGMOD 2001 Acceptance Rate

SIGMOD 2001 accepted 44 papers out of 293 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 15.0%.

ACM SIGMOD/PODS International Conference on Management of Data 2001 (27th) was held in Santa Barbara, California, USA.

Compared with SIGMOD 2000, the acceptance rate decreased from 16.9% to 15.0%, and submissions increased from 248 to 293.
