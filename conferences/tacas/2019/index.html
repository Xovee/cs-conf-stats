TACAS 2019 Acceptance Rate

TACAS 2019 accepted 29 papers out of 119 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.4%.

International Conference on Tools and Algorithms for the Construction and Analysis of Systems 2019 (25th) was held in Prague, Czech Republic.

Compared with TACAS 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 30.4% to 24.4%, and submissions increased from 115 to 119.
