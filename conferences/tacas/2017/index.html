TACAS 2017 Acceptance Rate

TACAS 2017 accepted 48 papers out of 167 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 28.7%.

International Conference on Tools and Algorithms for the Construction and Analysis of Systems 2017 (23rd) was held in Uppsala, Sweden.

Compared with TACAS 2016, the acceptance rate increased from 28.0% to 28.7%, and submissions increased from 157 to 167.
