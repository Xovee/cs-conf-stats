TACAS 2022 Acceptance Rate

TACAS 2022 accepted 33 papers out of 112 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.5%.

International Conference on Tools and Algorithms for the Construction and Analysis of Systems 2022 (28th) was held in Munich, Germany.

Compared with TACAS 2021, the acceptance rate decreased from 35.6% to 29.5%, and submissions increased from 90 to 112.
