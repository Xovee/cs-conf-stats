TACAS 2020 Acceptance Rate

TACAS 2020 accepted 31 papers out of 111 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.9%.

International Conference on Tools and Algorithms for the Construction and Analysis of Systems 2020 (26th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with TACAS 2019, the acceptance rate increased from 24.4% to 27.9%, and submissions decreased from 119 to 111.
