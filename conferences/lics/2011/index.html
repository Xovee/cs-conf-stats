LICS 2011 Acceptance Rate

LICS 2011 accepted 37 papers out of 114 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 32.5%.

ACM/IEEE Symposium on Logic in Computer Science 2011 (26th) was held in Toronto, Canada.

Compared with LICS 2010, the acceptance rate increased from 26.2% to 32.5%, and submissions decreased from 149 to 114.
