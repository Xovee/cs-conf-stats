LICS 1990 Acceptance Rate

LICS 1990 accepted 46 papers out of 185 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.9%.

ACM/IEEE Symposium on Logic in Computer Science 1990 (5th) was held in Philadelphia, USA.

Compared with LICS 1989, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.3% to 24.9%, and submissions increased from 139 to 185.
