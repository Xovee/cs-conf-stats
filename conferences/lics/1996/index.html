LICS 1996 Acceptance Rate

LICS 1996 accepted 47 papers out of 175 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 26.9%.

ACM/IEEE Symposium on Logic in Computer Science 1996 (11th) was held in New Brunswick, USA.

Compared with LICS 1995, the acceptance rate decreased from 32.1% to 26.9%, and submissions increased from 137 to 175.
