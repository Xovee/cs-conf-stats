LICS 2018 Acceptance Rate

LICS 2018 accepted 92 papers out of 217 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 42.4%.

ACM/IEEE Symposium on Logic in Computer Science 2018 (33rd) was held in Oxford, UK.

Compared with LICS 2017, the acceptance rate increased from 39.7% to 42.4%, and submissions decreased from 224 to 217.
