LICS 1993 Acceptance Rate

LICS 1993 accepted 38 papers out of 151 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.2%.

Compared with LICS 1990, the acceptance rate increased from 24.9% to 25.2%, and submissions decreased from 185 to 151.
