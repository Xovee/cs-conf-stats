KDD 2006 Acceptance Rate

KDD 2006 accepted 105 papers out of 457 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.0%.

ACM SIGKDD Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining 2006 (12th) was held in Philadelphia, USA.

Compared with KDD 2005, the acceptance rate increased from 16.3% to 23.0%, and submissions decreased from 465 to 457.
