KDD 2025 Acceptance Rate

KDD 2025 accepted 552 papers out of 2,955 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.7%.

ACM SIGKDD Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining 2025 (31th) was held in Toronto, Canada.

Compared with KDD 2024, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.1% to 18.7%, and submissions increased from 2,046 to 2,955.
