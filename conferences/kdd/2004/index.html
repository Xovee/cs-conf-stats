KDD 2004 Acceptance Rate

KDD 2004 accepted 85 papers out of 337 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 25.2%.

ACM SIGKDD Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining 2004 (10th) was held in Seattle, USA.

Compared with KDD 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 27.1% to 25.2%, and submissions increased from 258 to 337.
