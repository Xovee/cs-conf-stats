KDD 2003 Acceptance Rate

KDD 2003 accepted 70 papers out of 258 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 27.1%.

ACM SIGKDD Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining 2003 (9th) was held in Washington DC, USA.

Compared with KDD 2002, the acceptance rate increased from 25.6% to 27.1%, and submissions decreased from 270 to 258.
