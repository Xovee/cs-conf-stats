PPoPP 2009 Acceptance Rate

PPoPP 2009 accepted 26 papers out of 109 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.9%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles and Practice of Parallel Programming 2009 (14th) was held in Raleigh, USA.

Compared with PPoPP 2008, the acceptance rate decreased from 24.5% to 23.9%, and submissions increased from 102 to 109.
