PPoPP 2022 Acceptance Rate

PPoPP 2022 accepted 29 papers out of 118 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 24.6%.

ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles and Practice of Parallel Programming 2022 (27th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with PPoPP 2021, the acceptance rate increased from 20.7% to 24.6%, and submissions decreased from 150 to 118.
