CCS 2024 Acceptance Rate

CCS 2024 accepted 328 papers out of 1,964 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 16.7%.

ACM Conference on Computer and Communications Security 2024 (31st) was held in Salt Lake City, USA.

Compared with CCS 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.2% to 16.7%, and submissions increased from 1,222 to 1,964.
