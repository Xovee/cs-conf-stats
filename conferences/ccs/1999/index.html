CCS 1999 Acceptance Rate

CCS 1999 accepted 16 papers out of 83 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.3%.

ACM Conference on Computer and Communications Security 1999 (6th) was held in Singapore, Singapore.

Compared with CCS 1998, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.0% to 19.3%, and submissions decreased from 85 to 83.
