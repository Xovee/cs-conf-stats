CCS 2017 Acceptance Rate

CCS 2017 accepted 151 papers out of 836 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.1%.

ACM Conference on Computer and Communications Security 2017 (24th) was held in Dallas, USA.

Compared with CCS 2016, the acceptance rate increased from 16.5% to 18.1%, and submissions increased from 831 to 836.
