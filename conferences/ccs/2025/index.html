CCS 2025 Acceptance Rate

CCS 2025 accepted 316 papers out of 2,186 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 14.5%.

ACM Conference on Computer and Communications Security 2025 (32nd) was held in Taipei, Taiwan, China.

Compared with CCS 2024, the acceptance rate decreased from 16.7% to 14.5%, and submissions increased from 1,964 to 2,186.
