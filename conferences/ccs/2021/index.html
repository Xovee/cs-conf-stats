CCS 2021 Acceptance Rate

CCS 2021 accepted 196 papers out of 879 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 22.3%.

ACM Conference on Computer and Communications Security 2021 (28th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with CCS 2020, the acceptance rate increased from 16.9% to 22.3%, and submissions increased from 715 to 879.
