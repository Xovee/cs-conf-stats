Eurocrypt 1995 Acceptance Rate

Eurocrypt 1995 accepted 33 papers out of 113 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 29.2%.

Eurocrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Applications of Cryptographic Techniques 1995 (14th) was held in Saint-Malo, France.

Compared with Eurocrypt 1994, the acceptance rate increased from 26.3% to 29.2%, and submissions decreased from 137 to 113.
