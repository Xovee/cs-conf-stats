Eurocrypt 2024 Acceptance Rate

Eurocrypt 2024 accepted 105 papers out of 482 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.8%.

Eurocrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Applications of Cryptographic Techniques 2024 (43rd) was held in Zurich, Switzerland.

Compared with Eurocrypt 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 26.3% to 21.8%, and submissions increased from 415 to 482.
