Eurocrypt 2019 Acceptance Rate

Eurocrypt 2019 accepted 76 papers out of 327 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 23.2%.

Eurocrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Applications of Cryptographic Techniques 2019 (38th) was held in Darmstadt, Germany.

Compared with Eurocrypt 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.5% to 23.2%, and submissions increased from 294 to 327.
