Eurocrypt 2013 Acceptance Rate

Eurocrypt 2013 accepted 41 papers out of 202 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 20.3%.

Eurocrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Applications of Cryptographic Techniques 2013 (32nd) was held in Athens, Greece.

Compared with Eurocrypt 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 21.0% to 20.3%, and submissions increased from 195 to 202.
