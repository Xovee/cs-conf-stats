Eurocrypt 2014 Acceptance Rate

Eurocrypt 2014 accepted 38 papers out of 197 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.3%.

Eurocrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Applications of Cryptographic Techniques 2014 (33rd) was held in Copenhagen, Denmark.

Compared with Eurocrypt 2013, the acceptance rate decreased from 20.3% to 19.3%, and submissions decreased from 202 to 197.
