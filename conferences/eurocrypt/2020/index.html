Eurocrypt 2020 Acceptance Rate

Eurocrypt 2020 accepted 81 papers out of 375 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 21.6%.

Eurocrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Applications of Cryptographic Techniques 2020 (39th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with Eurocrypt 2019, the acceptance rate decreased from 23.2% to 21.6%, and submissions increased from 327 to 375.
