Eurocrypt 1993 Acceptance Rate

Eurocrypt 1993 accepted 36 papers out of 117 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 30.8%.

Eurocrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Applications of Cryptographic Techniques 1993 (12th) was held in Lofthus, Norway.

Compared with Eurocrypt 1990, the acceptance rate decreased from 49.4% to 30.8%, and submissions increased from 85 to 117.
