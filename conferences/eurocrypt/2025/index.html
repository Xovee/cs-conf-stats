Eurocrypt 2025 Acceptance Rate

Eurocrypt 2025 accepted 123 papers out of 602 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 20.4%.

Eurocrypt: International Conference on the Theory and Applications of Cryptographic Techniques 2025 (44th) was held in Madrid, Spain.

Compared with Eurocrypt 2024, the acceptance rate decreased from 21.8% to 20.4%, and submissions increased from 482 to 602.
