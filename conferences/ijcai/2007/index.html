IJCAI 2007 Acceptance Rate

IJCAI 2007 accepted 470 papers out of 1,365 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 34.4%.

International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence 2007 (20th) was held in Hyderabad, India.

Compared with IJCAI 2005, the acceptance rate increased from 17.9% to 34.4%, and submissions increased from 1,339 to 1,365.
