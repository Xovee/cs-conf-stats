IJCAI 2024 Acceptance Rate

IJCAI 2024 accepted 791 papers out of 5,651 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 14.0%.

International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence 2024 (33rd) was held in Jeju, Korea.

Compared with IJCAI 2023, the acceptance rate decreased from 14.1% to 14.0%, and submissions increased from 4,566 to 5,651.
