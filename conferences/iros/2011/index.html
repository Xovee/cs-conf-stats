IROS 2011 Acceptance Rate

IROS 2011 accepted 790 papers out of 2,459 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 32.1%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 2011 (24th) was held in San Francisco, California, USA.

Compared with IROS 2010, the acceptance rate decreased from 46.1% to 32.1%, and submissions increased from 1,798 to 2,459.
