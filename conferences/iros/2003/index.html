IROS 2003 Acceptance Rate

IROS 2003 accepted 600 papers out of 1,050 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 57.1%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 2003 (16th) was held in Las Vegas, USA.

Compared with IROS 2002, the acceptance rate decreased from 60.5% to 57.1%, and submissions increased from 826 to 1,050.
