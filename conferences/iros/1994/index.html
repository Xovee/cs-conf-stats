IROS 1994 Acceptance Rate

IROS 1994 accepted 299 papers out of 420 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 71.2%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 1994 (7th) was held in Munich, Germany.

Compared with IROS 1993, the acceptance rate decreased from 86.1% to 71.2%, and submissions increased from 360 to 420.
