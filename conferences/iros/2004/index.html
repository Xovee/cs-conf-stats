IROS 2004 Acceptance Rate

IROS 2004 accepted 659 papers out of 1,192 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 55.3%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 2004 (17th) was held in Sendai, Japan.

Compared with IROS 2003, the acceptance rate decreased from 57.1% to 55.3%, and submissions increased from 1,050 to 1,192.
