IROS 2021 Acceptance Rate

IROS 2021 accepted 1,301 papers out of 2,786 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 46.7%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 2021 (34th) was held in Online, Online.

Compared with IROS 2020, the acceptance rate decreased from 47.4% to 46.7%, and submissions decreased from 2,996 to 2,786.
