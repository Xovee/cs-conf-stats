IROS 1998 Acceptance Rate

IROS 1998 accepted 280 papers out of 476 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 58.8%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 1998 (11th) was held in Victoria, Canada.

Compared with IROS 1997, the acceptance rate decreased from 69.2% to 58.8%, and submissions increased from 396 to 476.
