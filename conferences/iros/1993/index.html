IROS 1993 Acceptance Rate

IROS 1993 accepted 310 papers out of 360 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 86.1%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 1993 (6th) was held in Yokohama, Japan.

Compared with IROS 1992, the acceptance rate increased from 66.7% to 86.1%, and submissions decreased from 366 to 360.
