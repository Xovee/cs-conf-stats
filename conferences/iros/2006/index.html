IROS 2006 Acceptance Rate

IROS 2006 accepted 971 papers out of 2,167 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 44.8%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 2006 (19th) was held in Beijing, China.

Compared with IROS 2005, the acceptance rate decreased from 55.1% to 44.8%, and submissions increased from 1,204 to 2,167.
