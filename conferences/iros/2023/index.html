IROS 2023 Acceptance Rate

IROS 2023 accepted 1,196 papers out of 2,760 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 43.3%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 2023 (36th) was held in Detroit, USA.

Compared with IROS 2022, the acceptance rate decreased from 47.9% to 43.3%, and submissions decreased from 3,579 to 2,760.
