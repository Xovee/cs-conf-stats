IROS 2013 Acceptance Rate

IROS 2013 accepted 904 papers out of 2,094 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 43.2%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 2013 (26th) was held in Tokyo, Japan.

Compared with IROS 2012, the acceptance rate decreased from 44.2% to 43.2%, and submissions increased from 1,825 to 2,094.
