IROS 1999 Acceptance Rate

IROS 1999 accepted 308 papers out of 439 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 70.2%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 1999 (12th) was held in Kyongju, Korea.

Compared with IROS 1998, the acceptance rate increased from 58.8% to 70.2%, and submissions decreased from 476 to 439.
