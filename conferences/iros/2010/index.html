IROS 2010 Acceptance Rate

IROS 2010 accepted 828 papers out of 1,798 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 46.1%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 2010 (23rd) was held in Taipei, Taiwan, China.

Compared with IROS 2009, the acceptance rate decreased from 58.5% to 46.1%, and submissions increased from 1,599 to 1,798.
