IROS 2024 Acceptance Rate

IROS 2024 accepted 1,600 papers out of 3,400 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 47.1%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 2024 (37th) was held in Abu Dhabi, UAE.

Compared with IROS 2023, the acceptance rate increased from 43.3% to 47.1%, and submissions increased from 2,760 to 3,400.
