IROS 2015 Acceptance Rate

IROS 2015 accepted 969 papers out of 2,134 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 45.4%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 2015 (28th) was held in Hamburg, Germany.

Compared with IROS 2014, the acceptance rate decreased from 46.9% to 45.4%, and submissions increased from 1,600 to 2,134.
