IROS 1995 Acceptance Rate

IROS 1995 accepted 270 papers out of 435 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 62.1%.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems 1995 (8th) was held in Pittsburgh, USA.

Compared with IROS 1994, the acceptance rate decreased from 71.2% to 62.1%, and submissions increased from 420 to 435.
