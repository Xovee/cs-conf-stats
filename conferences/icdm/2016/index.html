ICDM 2016 Acceptance Rate

ICDM 2016 accepted 178 papers out of 904 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.7%.

IEEE International Conference on Data Mining 2016 (16th) was held in Barcelona, Spain.

Compared with ICDM 2015, the acceptance rate increased from 18.1% to 19.7%, and submissions increased from 807 to 904.
