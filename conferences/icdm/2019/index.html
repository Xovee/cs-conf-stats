ICDM 2019 Acceptance Rate

ICDM 2019 accepted 194 papers out of 1,046 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 18.5%.

IEEE International Conference on Data Mining 2019 (19th) was held in Beijing, China.

Compared with ICDM 2018, the acceptance rate decreased from 19.8% to 18.5%, and submissions increased from 948 to 1,046.
