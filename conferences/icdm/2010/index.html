ICDM 2010 Acceptance Rate

ICDM 2010 accepted 155 papers out of 797 submissions, for an acceptance rate of 19.4%.

IEEE International Conference on Data Mining 2010 (10th) was held in Sydney, Australia.

Compared with ICDM 2009, the acceptance rate increased from 17.7% to 19.4%, and submissions increased from 786 to 797.
